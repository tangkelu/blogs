# Canonical Summary

> RT/duroid 6002 is a Rogers low-Dk, low-loss microwave laminate row that complements RT/duroid 5880 coverage when a more mechanically stable microwave material context is needed.

## Stable Facts

- Rogers identifies RT/duroid 6002 as a high-frequency laminate / microwave material.
- The datasheet lists process dielectric constant `2.94 +/- 0.04` in the Z direction at `10 GHz / 23 C` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.5`.
- The datasheet lists design Dk `2.94` across `8-40 GHz` by differential phase length method.
- The datasheet lists dissipation factor `0.0012` in the Z direction at `10 GHz / 23 C`.
- The datasheet lists TCDk `+12 ppm/C` from `0 C to 100 C` at `10 GHz`.
- The datasheet lists moisture absorption `0.02%`, thermal conductivity `0.60 W/m/K` at `80 C`, CTE `16 / 16 / 24 ppm/C` from `-55 C to 288 C`, Td `500 C`, density `2.1 g/cm3`, copper peel `1.6 N/mm`, flammability `V-0`, and lead-free process compatible `YES`.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep process Dk and design Dk scopes separate.
- Keep CTE range and thermal-conductivity temperature with the values.
- Treat the values as material properties, not finished-board microwave performance.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not prove insertion loss, antenna behavior, microwave package performance, or fabrication capability.
- It does not prove supplier capability, certification, yield, price, MOQ, or lead time.
