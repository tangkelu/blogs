# Canonical Summary

> `R-FR10` is an official Panasonic exact-product `FELIOS FRCC` row with condition-bound material values that can support guarded numeric writing for resin-coated-copper form-factor discussions.

## Stable Facts

- Panasonic publishes an official `FELIOS FRCC` page with a model-level property table for `R-FR10`.
- Panasonic describes `R-FR10` as `Flexible Resin-Coated Copper (FRCC)`.
- Panasonic publicly frames `R-FR10` as helping achieve thin multilayer circuit boards, with thin materials and good coplanarity that simplify the build-up process.
- `Dk at 1 GHz` is `3.0`.
- `Df at 1 GHz` is `0.019`.
- `Water absorption` is `1.2%` under `E-24/50 + D-24/23`.
- `Peel strength (1 Oz. Cu)` is `0.8 kN/m` with `ED:12 um`.
- `Tg (TMA)` is `190 C` for the adhesive layer.
- `CTE-Z <Tg` is `210 ppm/C` for the adhesive layer.
- `Volume resistivity` is `1 x 10^8 MOhm*cm` under `C-96/20/65`.
- `Sample thickness` is `0.032 mm` with `Cu:12 um, PI:5 um, Ad:15 um`.
- `Flammability` is `94VTM-0` measured by `R-FR10 / R-F775 25 um / R-FR10` construction.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep every value tied to the official Panasonic `FELIOS FRCC` model table for `R-FR10`.
- Keep adhesive-layer qualifiers such as `Ad` attached to `Tg` and `CTE-Z`.
- Use this card only as a product-grade `FRCC` / resin-coated-copper anchor, not as a generic rigid-board build-up default.
- Refresh before publication because the Panasonic page is dynamic and line-item values live on the product page rather than a fixed public PDF.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not prove that ordinary rigid multilayer boards should use `FRCC`.
- It does not provide dielectric-thickness windows, foil-option ladders, laser-via guidance, lamination-count rules, or qualification claims.
- It does not make `FRCC` interchangeable with `RCC`, `bond ply`, or ordinary prepreg.
- It does not turn Panasonic build-up-process wording into finished-board capability proof.
