# Canonical Summary

> `R-F705S` is an official Panasonic exact-product `FELIOS LCP` flexible-material row with usable high-frequency, moisture, dimensional-stability, and outgassing values when kept tied to the stated conditions.

## Stable Facts

- Panasonic publishes an official `FELIOS LCP` page for the exact product `R-F705S`.
- Panasonic positions `R-F705S` for high-speed large-volume mobile-data transmission and for antenna circuit boards that need water and environmental resistance.
- `Dk` is `2.9` at `14 GHz` by the balanced-type circular-disk resonator method under condition `A`.
- `Df` is `0.002` at `14 GHz` by the balanced-type circular-disk resonator method under condition `A`.
- `Dk` is `3.3` at `10 GHz` by the cavity resonator method under condition `A`.
- `Df` is `0.002` at `10 GHz` by the cavity resonator method under condition `A`.
- `Water absorption` is `0.04%` by internal method at `23 C` for `24 hr` immersion.
- `Peel strength` is `0.7 N/mm` with `ED:12 um` by `IPC-TM-650` under condition `A`.
- `Dimensional stability` after etching is `0.022%` in `MD` and `-0.007%` in `TD` by `IPC-TM-650`.
- `Dimensional stability` after `E-0.5/150` is `0.064%` in `MD` and `0.017%` in `TD` by `IPC-TM-650`.
- `Outgas TML` is `0.05%`, `CVCM` is `<0.01%`, and `WVR` is `0.04%` by `ASTM E595-07 / ASTM E595-15`.
- `Solder heat resistance` shows `No abnormality` by `IPC-TM-650` under `270 C solder float for 1 min`.
- The sample thickness for the listed values is `film 100 um, copper foil 12 um`.
- Panasonic states that the listed values are typical values and not guaranteed values.

- It does not prove that `R-F705S` is the material actually used by HIL/APT.
