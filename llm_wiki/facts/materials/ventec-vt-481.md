# Canonical Summary

> VT-481 is a Ventec mid-Tg FR-4 laminate / prepreg positioned as a phenolic-cure, lead-free-compatible material with low Z-CTE and CAF-resistance context. Its values must remain product-scoped and typical-value scoped.

## Stable Facts

- Ventec identifies VT-481 as a laminate / prepreg material with UL approval `E214381`, version `B5 /97 /98 /99 /101`.
- The property sheet lists `IPC-4101E /97 /98 /99 /101`.
- Ventec frames VT-481 around computer, communication equipment, instrumentation, electronic game machine, automotive, and similar applications.
- The sheet lists Tg `155 C` by DSC / `IPC-TM-650 2.4.25`.
- The same sheet lists Td `345 C` by `ASTM D3850`, T260 `>60 min`, T288 `25 min`, and thermal stress at `288 C` as `>600 s`.
- The same sheet lists Z-axis CTE before Tg `45 ppm/C`, after Tg `220 ppm/C`, total expansion from `50 C to 260 C` as `2.6%`, X-Y CTE `11~13 ppm/C`, and MOT `130 C`.
- The same sheet lists Dk `4.3` at `1 GHz`, `RC 50%`, by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.9`.
- The same sheet lists Df `0.015` at `1 GHz`, `RC 50%`, by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.9`.
- The same sheet lists moisture absorption `0.12%`, CTI `Grade 3 (175~250)`, arc resistance `147 s`, and flammability `V-0`.
- The sheet lists core thickness availability from `0.002 in / 0.05 mm` to `0.200 in / 5 mm`, copper foil availability from `1/4 oz` to `12 oz`, and prepregs in roll or panel form.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep Dk / Df attached to `1 GHz`, `RC 50%`, and `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.9`.
- Treat availability ranges as Ventec product availability context, not a supplier stock or build-capability claim.
- Treat all data as typical values, matching the source note.
- Do not promote process, packaging, bake, desmear, or storage guidance into generic shop process windows.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not prove lead-free assembly success, CAF lifetime, automotive qualification, or finished-board reliability.
- It does not prove a supplier can stock or fabricate every listed core, copper, or prepreg option.
- It does not prove APT, HIL, or any other supplier certification, yield, price, MOQ, stock, or lead time.
