# Canonical Summary

> `KB-6165LE` is an exact-product KBLaminates low-CTE FR-4 laminate / prepreg system with an official technical-information table.

## Stable Facts

- KBLaminates identifies `KB-6165LE` laminate with `KB-6065LE` prepreg.
- The official table lists `Tg 162 C`, Z-axis CTE total expansion `2.6%` from `50 to 260 C`, `T-260 >60 min`, `T-288 >40 min`, and `Td 355 C`.
- The table lists `Dk 4.6 @ 1 GHz` and `Df 0.016 @ 1 GHz`.
- The table lists CTI `>=300 V` and water absorption `0.08%`.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep all values tied to the official KBLaminates PDF.
- Treat values as typical reference values unless the source states a minimum or other bound.
- Keep `KB-6165LE` separated from `KB-6165`, `KB-6165F`, and `KB-6165C`.
