# Canonical Summary

> `LNB33C` is an exact-product Shengyi high-frequency antenna-grade material row. Its product-page values may be used as source-scoped material parameters, not as finished antenna, PIM, base-station, WiMAX, or supplier-capability claims.

## Stable Facts

- Shengyi publishes an official English product page for `LNB33C`.
- The page identifies LNB33C as a glass-reinforced hydrocarbon and ceramic dielectric with reduced-PIM positioning and lead-free process compatibility.
- The page lists Dk `3.30 +/- 0.05` in the Z direction at `C-24/23/50, 10 GHz` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.5`.
- The page lists Dk `3.56 +/- 0.05` in X/Y at `C-24/23/50, 10 GHz` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.15`.
- The page lists Df `0.0030` in X/Y at `C-24/23/50, 10 GHz` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.15`.
- The page lists thermal coefficient of Dk `+50 ppm/C` in the Z direction over `-55 to 85 C` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.5`.
- The page lists a PIM row of `-158 dBc` under `43 dBm`, `1900 MHz`, `50 ohm`, `0.0307"` test context.
- The page lists volume resistivity `6.8 x 10^8 MOhm-cm`, surface resistivity `5.3 x 10^7 MOhm`, flexural strength `200 / 160 MPa` in X/Y, CTE `55 ppm/C` in Z from `50 to 260 C`, and CTE `12 / 12 ppm/C` in X/Y from `50 to 260 C`.
- The page lists Tg `>280 C` by DSC, Td `400 C` at `5% wt. loss`, thermal conductivity `0.65 W/(m.K)` in Z at `50 C`, water absorption `0.05%`, peel strength `1.05 N/mm [6.0 lb/in]` with `1 oz HVLP` at `288 C / 10 s`, and flammability `V-0`.
- The page states typical values are based on a `0.780 mm (0.0307")` thickness specimen, are for reference, and are not intended as specifications.

## Conditions And Methods

- Preserve direction and method for every Dk / Df / CTE value.
- Treat the PIM row as source-specific material-test context, not a finished-board or antenna performance guarantee.
- Preserve the specimen-thickness caveat when using values in comparison tables.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not prove finished antenna performance, base-station qualification, WiMAX performance, PIM for a manufactured PCB, insertion loss, RF compliance, or production qualification.
- It does not prove APT, HIL, or any supplier's material stock, process capability, certification, procurement approval, price, MOQ, lead time, or yield.
