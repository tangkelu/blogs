# Canonical Summary

> N4000-29NF is an AGC no-flow bond ply based on the N4000-29 resin system. Its reusable value is the exact-product no-flow prepreg material form and source-scoped properties, not a generic lamination recipe.

## Stable Facts

- AGC describes N4000-29NF as a no-flow bond ply based on the N4000-29 resin system.
- The TDS states N4000-29NF is designed for bonding flex circuitry and heat sinks to rigid circuit boards, with minimal and consistent resin flow.
- The available-prepreg table lists glass style `106`, `65% RC`, flow `50-120 mils`, thickness `0.0017 inch`, Dk `3.84 at 2 GHz`, and Df `0.0194 at 2 GHz`.
- The same table lists glass style `1080`, `61% RC`, flow `50-120 mils`, thickness `0.0029 inch`, Dk `3.94 at 2 GHz`, and Df `0.0189 at 2 GHz`.
- The available-prepreg table states flow is tested per `IPC-TM-650 2.3.17.2`.
- The main property table lists dielectric constant of `3.89 at 2 GHz` and `3.81 at 10 GHz` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.5`.
- The same TDS lists dissipation factor of `0.0192 at 2 GHz` and `0.0205 at 10 GHz`.
- The same TDS lists volume resistivity `8.10 x 10^7 MOhm-cm` at `C-96 / 35 / 90` and `1.90 x 10^8 MOhm-cm` at `E-24 / 125`.
- The same TDS lists surface resistivity `5.60 x 10^6 MOhm` at `C-96 / 35 / 90` and `1.80 x 10^7 MOhm` at `E-24 / 125`.
- The same TDS lists electric strength `4.2 x 10^4 V/mm` / `1100 V/mil`.
- The same TDS lists Tg `199 C` by DMA `Tan d Peak`.
- The same TDS lists degradation temperature `350 C` at `5%` weight loss and `T-260 > 60 minutes`.
- The same TDS lists thermal conductivity `0.46 W/mK`.
- The same TDS lists X/Y CTE from `-40 C to +125 C` of `12 / 15 ppm/C`, Z-axis CTE Alpha 1 / Alpha 2 at `55% RC` of `55 / 265 ppm/C`, and Z-axis expansion at `43% RC` of `3.0%` from `50 C to 260 C`.
- The same TDS lists moisture absorption `0.15 wt.%`.
- The TDS states N4000-29NF meets `UL 94V-0` and `IPC-4101 /98`, `/99`, `/126`, and `/129`, with UL file number `E36295`.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep the construction-table rows separate from the main property-table rows.
- Preserve glass style, resin content, thickness, flow, frequency, and test method when reusing the available-prepreg values.
- Treat all listed values as typical datasheet values, not guaranteed specification limits.
- The TDS includes vendor lamination guidance; do not use it as a generic shop process window or proof that any specific rigid-flex or heat-sink build will pass.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not prove a supplier can bond a specific rigid-flex stackup, attach a specific heat sink, meet a flow target, or pass a released-lot qualification.
- It does not prove high-layer-count reliability, multiple-reflow survival, repair-operation success, IST performance, CAF lifetime, or plated-through-hole reliability for a finished board.
- It does not prove APT, HIL, or any other supplier capability, certification, yield, lead time, stock, price, or qualification status.
- It does not provide a reusable lamination recipe for suppliers or designs.
