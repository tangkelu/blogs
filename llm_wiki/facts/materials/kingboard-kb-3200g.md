# Canonical Summary

> `KB-3200G` is an exact-product KBLaminates halogen-free low-Dk/Df laminate and prepreg system with an official technical-information table, but its material properties do not by themselves prove any specific SerDes, Ethernet, PAM4, or backplane channel outcome.

## Stable Facts

- KBLaminates identifies `KB-3200G` laminate with `PP-KB3200G` prepreg and references `UL E123995`.
- The source describes the material as halogen, antimony, and red phosphorous free, with low Dk/Df, low Z-CTE, and anti-CAF capability.
- The official table references `IPC-4101E/130`.
- For the stated specimen scope, the table lists thermal stress `>=240 sec` at `Float 288 C / Unetched`, `Tg 178 C` by DSC, `Tg 193 C` by DMA, `T-260 >60 min`, `T-288 >60 min`, and `Td 387 C`.
- The table lists Z-axis CTE `Alpha 1 45 ppm/C`, `Alpha 2 200 ppm/C`, total expansion `1.8%` from `50 to 260 C`, and X/Y CTE `12 / 15 ppm/C` from `40 to 125 C`.
- The table lists flammability `V-0`.
- The table lists `Dk 4.1 @ 1 GHz` and `Dk 4.0 @ 10 GHz`, plus `Df 0.0075 @ 1 GHz` and `Df 0.0085 @ 10 GHz`, by `IEC 61189-2-721`, etched, `RC50%`.
- The table lists CTI `>=175 V`, dielectric breakdown `>=45 kV`, arc resistance `122 sec`, water absorption `0.11%`, and flexural strength `580 / 490 N/mm2`.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep the specimen note attached: typical value of specimen thickness is `1.0 mm`, `#2116 x 10`.
- Treat all values as typical reference values unless the source states a minimum or other bound.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not prove 10G, 25G, 56G, 112G, PCIe, USB, Ethernet, PAM4, insertion-loss, eye-margin, or trace-length outcomes.
- It does not authorize hybrid-stackup savings, HVLP requirements, backdrill thresholds, pre-stock, lead-time, cost, or HIL/APT capability claims.
