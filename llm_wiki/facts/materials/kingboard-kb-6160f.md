# Canonical Summary

> `KB-6160F` is an exact-product KBLaminates low-CTE FR-4 laminate / prepreg system with an official technical-information table.

## Stable Facts

- KBLaminates identifies `KB-6160F` laminate with `KB-6060F` prepreg.
- The official table lists `Tg 135 C`, Z-axis CTE `Alpha 1 42 ppm/C`, total expansion `4.0%` from `50 to 260 C`, `T-260 >10 min`, and `Td 313 C`.
- The table lists `Dk 4.7 @ 1 MHz` and `Dk 4.6 @ 1 GHz`, plus `Df 0.015 @ 1 MHz` and `Df 0.016 @ 1 GHz`.
- The table lists CTI `>=175 V` and water absorption `0.13%`.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep all values tied to the official KBLaminates PDF.
- Treat values as typical reference values unless the source states a minimum or other bound.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not prove drill-cost savings, impedance shift, layer-count suitability, HIL/APT capability, cost, lead time, or yield.
- It does not validate KB-6160, KB-6160A, KB-6160LC, or KB-6160LC(C).
