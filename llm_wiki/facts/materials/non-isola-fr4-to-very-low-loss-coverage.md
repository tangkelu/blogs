# Canonical Summary

> `llm_wiki` now has official non-Isola public anchors across a broader digital laminate ladder: exact-product high-Tg FR-4 examples, lower-loss multilayer examples, and very-low-loss / ultra-low-loss networking-backplane examples from Shengyi, ITEQ, Doosan, and EMC.

## Stable Facts

- `Shengyi S1000-2M` adds an official high-Tg multilayer FR-4 product anchor.
- `Shengyi S7439` adds an official lower-loss multilayer / backplane anchor.
- `ITEQ IT-150DA` adds an official high-Tg low-loss digital laminate anchor.
- `ITEQ IT-968` adds an official very-low-loss digital laminate anchor with separate exact-product values.
- `ITEQ IT-968G` remains an adjacent official very-low-loss anchor that should stay separate from `IT-968`.
- `ITEQ IT-968SE`, `IT-988GSE`, `IT-988GL`, and `IT-988G` add official ultra-low-loss product-grade anchors that should stay separated by exact grade name rather than flattened into one ITEQ row.
- `Doosan DS-7402` adds an official mainstream halogen-free FR-4 product anchor.
- `Doosan DS-7409DQN` adds an official super-ultra-low-loss networking / backplane anchor.
- EMC's official notebook-material page adds another public product-table anchor for halogen-free and lower-loss / very-low-loss laminate examples.
- `MEGTRON M` adds an official Panasonic product-grade anchor with condition-bound material values and explicit `high multi-layer` / `IST` context for conservative high-layer digital-material framing.
- `ThunderClad 5Q` adds an official TUC product-grade very-low-loss laminate / prepreg anchor with condition-bound values plus explicit `high layer count circuit board design` positioning.

## Conditions And Methods

- Use this card when a layer-count article needs broader supplier examples beyond Isola and Panasonic.
- Pair this card with exact supplier pages before carrying any numeric value into a draft.
- Keep `MEGTRON M` tied to its own product-page conditions and note that the listed values are typical values rather than guaranteed board outcomes.
- Keep `ThunderClad 5Q` tied to its own page conditions and note that the listed values are typical values rather than guaranteed board outcomes.
- Use these products as vendor-typical examples, not as universal design requirements.
- For ITEQ-specific ordering and non-flattening guardrails, use the dedicated ITEQ ladder normalization card rather than this cross-vendor aggregate card.
- For cross-vendor high-speed digital placement, use the dedicated digital-material ladder normalization card rather than turning this aggregate coverage card into a flat ranking table.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not create a universal vendor ranking.
- It does not claim these products are interchangeable or stocked.
- It does not convert vendor-page insertion-loss lines or application tables into guaranteed board outcomes.
- It does not turn Panasonic `high multi-layer` or `IST` context graphics into build-sequence rules, interconnect qualification, or fabricator capability claims.
