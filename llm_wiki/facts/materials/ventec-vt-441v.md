# Canonical Summary

> VT-441V is a Ventec phenolic-cured, halogen-free, mid-Tg FR-4-family laminate / prepreg with high-Td, UV-blocking, CAF-resistance, and FR15.1 / MOT context. Its data must remain product-scoped and typical-value scoped.

## Stable Facts

- Ventec identifies VT-441V as a laminate / prepreg with UL approval `E214381`, version `A1`, IPC-4101E WAM1 `/127 /128 /153`.
- Ventec frames VT-441V around mobile phone, smart phone, automotive, LED lighting, high-power application, computer, instrumentation, communication equipment, electronic game machine, and VCR contexts.
- The sheet lists Tg `152 C` by DSC / `IPC-TM-650 2.4.25` and Tg `158 C` by TMA / `IPC-TM-650 2.4.24`.
- The same sheet lists Td `385 C` by `ASTM D3850`, T260 `>60 min`, T288 `>30 min`, and thermal stress at `288 C` as `>600 s`.
- The same sheet lists Z-axis CTE before Tg `38 ppm/C`, after Tg `190 ppm/C`, total expansion from `50 C to 260 C` as `2.2%`, X-Y CTE `11~13 ppm/C`, and MOT `150 C`.
- At `RC 50%`, the sheet lists Dk `4.4` at `1 GHz` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.9`.
- At `RC 50%`, the sheet lists Df `0.014` at `1 GHz` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.9`.
- The same sheet lists moisture absorption `0.18%`, CTI `Grade 3 (175~250)`, arc resistance `187 s`, and flammability `V-0`.
- The sheet lists core thickness availability from `0.002 in / 0.05 mm` to `0.200 in / 5 mm`, copper foil availability from `1/4 oz` to `12 oz`, and multiple E-glass styles.

## Conditions And Methods

- Preserve `RC 50%`, `1 GHz`, and IPC-TM-650 `2.5.5.9` on Dk / Df values.
- Treat all data as typical values, matching the source note.
- Treat availability ranges and reverse-treated-copper guidance as Ventec product context, not supplier stock or generic process-window proof.
