# Canonical Summary

> `Kapton HN` is an exact-product DuPont all-polyimide film anchor. It can support source-scoped polyimide film discussion, but it does not by itself authorize flex PCB bend-life, bend-radius, rigid-flex stackup, or finished-board reliability claims.

## Stable Facts

- DuPont publishes an official product page and data sheet for `Kapton HN`.
- DuPont describes `Kapton HN` as a general-purpose all-polyimide film.
- DuPont states that `Kapton HN` has been used successfully from `-269 C` to `400 C`.
- DuPont states that `Kapton HN` film can be laminated, metallized, punched, formed, or adhesive coated.
- The product page and data sheet identify applications including electronic parts, electrical insulation, pressure-sensitive tape, fiber optics cable, insulation blankets, insulation tubing, automotive diaphragms, etching, and shims.
- The product page lists physical-property rows for common `1 mil`, `2 mil`, `3 mil`, and `5 mil` film thicknesses.
- The product page lists ultimate tensile strength at `73 F` as `231 MPa` across the shown thicknesses by `ASTM D-882-91 Method A`.
- The product page lists ultimate tensile strength at `392 F` as `139 MPa` across the shown thicknesses by `ASTM D-882-91 Method A`.
- The product page lists density as `1.42 g/cc` by `ASTM D-1505-90`.
- The product page lists tensile modulus at `73 F` as `2.76 GPa` and at `392 F` as `2.0 GPa`.
- The DuPont data sheet states that `Kapton HN` meets `ASTM D-5213 type 1 item A` requirements.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep all values tied to DuPont's `Kapton HN` page or data sheet.
- Preserve film thickness, temperature, and test method whenever using physical-property rows.
- Use this card when a draft needs a verified `Kapton HN` product example rather than generic `Kapton`, `polyimide`, or `PI` wording.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not make `Kapton HN` interchangeable with all `Kapton` grades, all polyimide films, `UPILEX`, `LCP`, Panasonic `R-F705S`, Arlon `85NT`, or rigid-flex laminate systems.
- It does not provide flex PCB bend-radius, bend-cycle, copper-type, coverlay, adhesive, or stackup rules.
- It does not prove HIL/APT material availability, production use, supplier approval, IPC-6013 compliance, Class 3 status, lot acceptance, cost, lead time, or yield.
- It does not turn film-level temperature or tensile data into finished-board reliability proof.
