# Canonical Summary

> `KB-6167GLD` is an exact-product KBLaminates low-loss laminate / prepreg system with official material-property coverage. It does not by itself validate PAM4, PCIe, Ethernet, loss-budget, or trace-length claims.

## Stable Facts

- KBLaminates identifies `KB-6167GLD` laminate with `KB-6067GLD` prepreg.
- The official table lists `Tg 220 C` by DMA, `Td 409 C`, and Z-axis expansion `1.8%` from `50 to 260 C`.
- The table lists `Dk 3.9 @ 1 GHz` and `Dk 3.8 @ 10 GHz`, plus `Df 0.006 @ 1 GHz` and `Df 0.007 @ 10 GHz`.
- The table lists CTI `>=175 V`.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep all values tied to the official KBLaminates PDF.
- Treat values as typical reference values unless the source states a minimum or other bound.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not prove PCIe Gen 5, 25G, 56G, PAM4, Ethernet, insertion-loss budgets, VLP/HVLP rules, Megtron equivalence, cost, lead time, or HIL/APT capability.
