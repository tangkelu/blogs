# Canonical Summary

> `S1150G` is an exact-product Shengyi halogen-free mid-`Tg` FR-4 anchor. Its values may be used as source-scoped material parameters, not as generic halogen-free FR-4 averages or finished-board capability claims.

## Stable Facts

- Shengyi publishes an official exact-product page for `S1150G`.
- The page positions `S1150G` under halogen-free and lead-free-compatible `FR-4.1 / FR-15.1` rigid materials.
- Shengyi describes `S1150G` as free of halogen, antimony, and red phosphorous constituents, with lead-free process compliance.
- The page lists application context including smartphone, tablet, notebook, LED, game station, automotive electronics, and communication equipment.
- The table lists `Tg 155 C` by `DSC` under `IPC-TM-650 2.4.25`.
- The table lists `Td 355 C` at `5% wt. loss` by `IPC-TM-650 2.4.24.6`.
- The table lists `Z-axis CTE 40 ppm/C` before `Tg`, `230 ppm/C` after `Tg`, and `2.8%` from `50 to 260 C`.
- The table lists `T260 60 min` and `T288 45 min` by `TMA`.
- The table lists thermal stress at `288 C` solder dip as `>100 s no delamination`.
- The table lists `Dk 4.5` at `1 GHz` and `4.8` at `1 MHz` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.9`.
- The table lists `Df 0.011` at `1 GHz` and `0.009` at `1 MHz` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.9`.
- The page states that typical values are reference values and are not intended as specifications.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep all numeric values tied to the official `S1150G` product page and its stated methods.
- Preserve frequency whenever using `Dk` or `Df`.
- Treat the application list as material positioning context, not as proof that a finished board meets automotive, communication, LED, or consumer-device requirements.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not make `S1150G` interchangeable with `S1000-2`, `S1000-2M`, `S1150GH`, `S1170G`, or all Shengyi halogen-free FR-4 materials.
- It does not create a generic halogen-free FR-4 average row.
- It does not prove HIL or APT material availability, procurement approval, process capability, stackup suitability, lead time, cost, or yield.
- It does not authorize process-window, lamination-cycle, drilling, registration, or board-reliability claims.
