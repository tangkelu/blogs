# Canonical Summary

> Arlon TC350 and TC600 are thermally enhanced microwave laminates offering exceptional thermal conductivity to pull heat away from active RF components while maintaining stable Dk and low loss.

## Stable Facts

### Electrical Properties
- **Dk**: 3.50 (TC350) - 6.15 (TC600)
- **Df**: 0.0020

### Thermal Properties
- **Thermal Conductivity**: 1.0 W/m·K (TC350)
- **Purpose**: Heat spreading from active RF components
- **Dk Stability**: Maintained under thermal load

### Key Characteristics
- Thermally enhanced PTFE / woven glass / ceramic
- Exceptional thermal conductivity for RF substrate
- Maintains stable Dk and low loss
- Requires plasma desmear

### Applications
- High-power amplifiers (PA)
- Tower-mounted amplifiers (TMA)
- GaN and GaAs device mounting
- Broadcast transmitters

## Conditions And Methods

- Stocked at APTPCB
- Thermal vias and planar heat spreading recommended
- GaN device junction temperature benefits from TC350 substrate

## Limits And Non-Claims

- Thermal performance depends on overall thermal design
- Does not eliminate need for heat sinks in high-power applications
- Device temperature depends on mounting method
