# Canonical Summary

> Shengyi high-Tg FR-4 rows should stay product-specific. `S1000-2` and `S1000-2M` now both have exact-product public tables, but they still should not collapse into one generic Shengyi row.

## Stable Facts

- `S1000-2` now has its own official exact-product page and public property table.
- `S1000-2M` also has its own official exact-product page and public property table.
- Product-grade numeric fields should be attached to the exact product and page conditions.
- This card exists to keep Shengyi rows comparison-ready without flattening them into one family bucket.

## Conditions And Methods

- Use this card for exact-product FR-4 cleanup in `H1`, not for family-level flattening.
- Keep `Tg`, `Td`, `Dk`, `Df`, `CTE`, `T288`, and moisture fields product-specific.
- Keep the `S1000-2` page-level `Tg` wording discrepancy visible if numeric rows are compared.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- Do not promote catalog-level discovery into exact numeric reuse.
- Do not treat one Shengyi page as a proxy for all Shengyi FR-4 systems.
