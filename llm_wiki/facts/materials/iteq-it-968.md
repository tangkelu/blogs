# Canonical Summary

> `IT-968` is now an exact-product ITEQ very-low-loss laminate anchor with separate official page and datasheet support, while remaining explicitly distinct from adjacent `IT-968G`.

## Stable Facts

- ITEQ publishes an official product page that names `IT-968` and `IT-968G`.
- The `IT-968` section has its own exact-product property table and linked `IT-968` datasheet PDF.
- The page lists `Tg 190 C` by `TMA`.
- The page lists `T288 >60 min` with `1 oz Cu` by `TMA`.
- The page lists `Td 400 C` by `TGA 5% loss`.
- The page lists `CTE 45/260 ppm/C` as `a1/a2`.
- The page lists `CTE 2.2%` from `50 to 260 C` by `TMA`.
- The page lists `Dk 3.14` at `10 GHz` with `RC 70%` by `IPC TM-650 2.5.5.13`.
- The page lists `Df 0.0039` at `10 GHz` with `RC 70%` by `IPC TM-650 2.5.5.13`.
- The datasheet also provides multi-frequency dielectric values, moisture absorption `0.1%`, peel strength `2.5-3.5 lb/in`, `UL94 V-0`, `CTI 3 (175-249)`, `MOT 130 C`, in-plane `CTE 12/14 ppm/C`, and resistivity context.
- The official page and datasheet keep `IT-968` distinct from `IT-968G` and `IT-968SE`, whose values and positioning are different.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep all numeric values tied to the official `IT-968` page section and linked `IT-968` datasheet.
- Keep dielectric values tied to their stated frequency and resin-content conditions, especially `10 GHz` and `RC 70%`.
- Preserve the distinction between `IT-968`, `IT-968G`, `IT-968SE`, `IT-968TC`, and `IT-968BS` where the official sources name them separately.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not make `IT-968` interchangeable with `IT-968G`, `IT-968SE`, `IT-602G`, `IT-988GLSE`, or other very-low-loss laminates.
- It does not turn wireless, radar, or antenna wording into guaranteed RF or board-level SI performance.
- It does not prove a specific layer-count capability, sequential-lamination recipe, or manufacturability result.
