# Canonical Summary

> `55NT` is an official Arlon exact-product epoxy laminate and prepreg system with non-woven aramid reinforcement, useful in `llm_wiki` as a controlled-CTE / SMT material anchor when all values stay tied to the official datasheet.

## Stable Facts

- Arlon publishes an official product page, official datasheet, and official processing guide for `55NT`.
- The official source posture is strong enough to treat `55NT` as an exact-product baseline anchor rather than a family-only mention.
- The datasheet identifies `55NT` as an epoxy laminate and prepreg system reinforced with a non-woven aramid substrate.
- The datasheet headline block states low in-plane `(X,Y)` expansion of `6-9 ppm/C`.
- The same datasheet headline block states `Tg 170 C`, `decomposition temperature 368 C`, and `Z-expansion 3.5% between 50-260 C`.
- The same datasheet says `55NT` meets the requirements of `IPC-4101/55`.
- The same datasheet states compatibility with lead-free processing.
- The same datasheet positions `55NT` for military and commercial avionics, missiles and missile defense, satellites, and other high-reliability SMT applications that need low in-plane CTE values.
- The official product tree places `55NT` under `Controlled Thermal Expansion/SMT`.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep every numeric value tied to the official `55NT` datasheet wording and units.
- Use the processing guide only for guarded process-discipline context.
- Treat application wording such as `high-reliability SMT` and `controlled CTE` as manufacturer positioning, not as finished-board qualification proof.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not prove a specific microvia capability, stackup, or fabrication window.
- It does not turn application wording into universal aerospace, military, or satellite qualification status.
- It does not replace design-specific thermal, reliability, or assembly validation.
