# Canonical Summary

> `S1000-2` is an exact-product Shengyi high-Tg FR-4 anchor with a public property table strong enough for baseline numeric recovery, provided the page-level `Tg` wording and the table values are kept visibly distinct.

## Stable Facts

- Shengyi publishes an official exact-product page for `S1000-2`.
- The product page positions `S1000-2` as a lead-free-compatible `FR-4.0` laminate with low `Z-axis CTE`, strong through-hole reliability, `CAF` resistance, and low water absorption.
- The product page characteristic block says `Tg 170 C (DSC)`.
- The product-performance table lists `Tg 180 C` by `DSC` and `185 C` by `DMA`.
- The table lists `Td 345 C` by `IPC-TM-650 2.4.24.6` at `5% wt. loss`.
- The table lists `CTE (Z-axis) 45 ppm/C` before `Tg`, `220 ppm/C` after `Tg`, and `2.8%` from `50 to 260 C`.
- The table lists `T260 60 min`, `T288 20 min`, and `T300 5 min` by `IPC-TM-650 2.4.24.1`.
- The table lists `Dk 4.8` at `1 MHz` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.9`.
- The table lists `Df 0.013` at `1 MHz` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.9`.
- The table lists `water absorption 0.10%`, `peel strength 1.38 N/mm` after thermal stress at `288 C, 10 s`, and flammability `V-0`.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep numeric values tied to the official `S1000-2` product-performance table and its stated methods/conditions.
- Keep the page-level `Tg 170 C` marketing line visible if `S1000-2` is used in comparative work, because the table itself lists `Tg 180 C (DSC)`.
- Do not fabricate `10 GHz` dielectric values from the blank `10 GHz` rows on the public page.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not make `S1000-2` interchangeable with `S1000-2M` or every Shengyi `FR-4` system.
- It does not prove a specific high-layer-count capability, router qualification, or board-level SI outcome.
- It does not turn page-level application wording into a universal design recommendation.
