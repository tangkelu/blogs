# Canonical Summary

> VT-90H is a Ventec high-Tg polyimide laminate / prepreg with high-Td and low-Z-axis-CTE positioning. Its application language must not be treated as qualification proof.

## Stable Facts

- Ventec identifies VT-90H as a laminate / prepreg with UL approval `E214381`, version `08/01/2025`, IPC-4101E `/40 /41`.
- Ventec frames VT-90H around chip manufacturers, engine / flight controls, power supply / backplane, military and aerospace, burn-in board, and downhole drilling contexts.
- The sheet lists Tg `250 C` by TMA / `IPC-TM-650 2.4.24`.
- The same sheet lists Td `408 C` by `ASTM D3850`, T260 `>60 min`, T288 `>60 min`, and thermal stress at `288 C` as `>1200 s`.
- The same sheet lists Z-axis CTE before Tg `50 ppm/C`, after Tg `145 ppm/C`, total expansion from `50 C to 260 C` as `1.2%`, X/Y CTE `11~12 ppm/C`, and MOT `140 C`.
- At `RC 40%`, the sheet lists dielectric constant `4.05` at `1 GHz` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.9`.
- At `RC 40%`, the sheet lists dissipation factor `0.012` at `1 GHz` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.9`.
- The same sheet lists moisture absorption `0.28%`, CTI `Grade 4 (100-175)`, arc resistance `150 s`, and flammability `HB`.
- The sheet lists core thickness availability from `0.002 in / 0.05 mm` to `0.125 in / 3 mm`, copper foil availability from `1/4 oz` to `6 oz`, and E-glass styles including `7628`, `1506`, `2113`, `2313`, `3313`, `2116`, `1080`, and `106`.

## Conditions And Methods

- Preserve `RC 40%`, `1 GHz`, and IPC-TM-650 `2.5.5.9` on Dk / Df values.
