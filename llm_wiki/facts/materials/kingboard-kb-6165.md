# Canonical Summary

> `KB-6165` is an exact-product KBLaminates lead-free / Tg 150 / low-CTE FR-4 laminate and prepreg system. The official PDF resolves the draft conflict by listing `Td 348 C` for the current source-scoped card.

## Stable Facts

- KBLaminates identifies `KB-6165` laminate with `KB-6065` prepreg and references `UL E123995`.
- The official table references `IPC-4101E/99/101`.
- For the stated specimen scope, the table lists thermal stress `>=240 sec` at `Float 288 C / Unetched`, `Tg 153 C` by `IPC-TM-650 2.4.25 DSC`, `T-260 >60 min`, `T-288 >20 min`, and `Td 348 C`.
- The table lists Z-axis CTE `Alpha 1 42 ppm/C`, `Alpha 2 240 ppm/C`, total expansion `3.0%` from `50 to 260 C`, and X/Y CTE `12 / 15 ppm/C` from `40 to 125 C`.
- The table lists flammability `V-0`.
- The table lists `Dk 4.8 @ 1 MHz` and `Dk 4.6 @ 1 GHz`, plus `Df 0.015 @ 1 MHz` and `Df 0.016 @ 1 GHz`, by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.9`, etched, `RC50%`.
- The table lists CTI `>=175 V`, dielectric breakdown `>=45 kV`, arc resistance `120 sec`, water absorption `0.09%`, and flexural strength `550 / 470 N/mm2`.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep the specimen note attached: typical value of specimen thickness is `1.6 mm`, `#7628 x 8`.
- Treat all values as typical reference values unless the source states a minimum or other bound.
- Keep `KB-6165` separated from `KB-6165F`, `KB-6165C`, and `KB-6165LE`.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not prove Gbps/GHz channel suitability, service life, trace-length rules, layer count, availability, inventory, lead time, or no-redesign substitution.
- It does not authorize cost, supplier-status, or HIL/APT capability claims.
