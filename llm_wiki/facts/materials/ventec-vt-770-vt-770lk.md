# Canonical Summary

> VT-770 and VT-770(LK) are Ventec tec-speed 6.0 / 6.1 H-PK laminate / prepreg products with split low-Dk and low-Df values. Keep the two product columns separate.

## Stable Facts

- Ventec identifies the paired sheet as `tec-speed 6.0 / 6.1 H-PK - VT-770 / VT-770(LK)` laminate / prepreg with UL approval `E214381`, version `A01`.
- Ventec frames the pair around handheld equipment, high-frequency and high-speed applications, satellite communications, navigation, GPS, and LTE.
- The sheet lists Tg by TMA `230 C` for both `VT-770` and `VT-770(LK)` and Tg by DMA `260 C` for both.
- The same sheet lists Td at `5%` weight loss `450 C`, T260 `>60 min`, T288 `>60 min`, and thermal stress at `288 C` as `>600 s` for both products.
- The same sheet lists Z-axis CTE before Tg `25 ppm/C` for `VT-770` and `22 ppm/C` for `VT-770(LK)`, plus XY-axis CTE `9~10 ppm/C` for both.
- At `RC 50%`, the sheet lists Dk at `1 GHz` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.9` of `3.5~3.6` for `VT-770` and `3.4~3.5` for `VT-770(LK)`.
- At `RC 50%`, the sheet lists Dk at `10 GHz` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.13` of `3.4~3.5` for `VT-770` and `3.3~3.4` for `VT-770(LK)`.
- At `RC 50%`, the sheet lists Df at `1 GHz` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.9` of `0.0030` for `VT-770` and `0.0020` for `VT-770(LK)`.
- At `RC 50%`, the sheet lists Df at `10 GHz` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.13` of `0.0040` for `VT-770` and `0.0024` for `VT-770(LK)`.
- The same sheet lists moisture absorption `0.1%`, CTI `Grade 3 (175~250)`, arc resistance `195 s`, and flammability `V-0` for both products.
- The sheet lists core thickness availability from `0.0012 in / 0.03 mm` to `0.031 in / 0.8 mm`, copper foil from `1/7 oz / 5 um` to `2 oz / 70 um`, and prepreg thickness `0.02 mm` to `0.1 mm`.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep `VT-770` and `VT-770(LK)` columns separate.
- Preserve `RC 50%` on Dk / Df values.
- Keep `1 GHz` IPC-TM-650 `2.5.5.9` rows separate from `10 GHz` IPC-TM-650 `2.5.5.13` rows.
- Treat all data as typical values, matching the source note.
