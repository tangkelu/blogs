# Canonical Summary

> `HF-140` is an exact-product KBLaminates halogen-free / normal-Tg / low-CTE laminate and prepreg system with an official technical-information table.

## Stable Facts

- KBLaminates identifies `HF-140` laminate with `PP-HF140` prepreg and references `UL E123995`.
- The source describes the material as halogen, antimony, and red phosphorous free.
- The official table references `IPC-4101E/127`.
- For the stated specimen scope, the table lists thermal stress `>=240 sec` at `Float 288 C / Unetched`, `Tg 141 C` by DSC, `T-260 >60 min`, `T-288 >5 min`, and `Td 350 C`.
- The table lists Z-axis CTE `Alpha 1 45 ppm/C`, `Alpha 2 254 ppm/C`, total expansion `3.3%` from `50 to 260 C`, and X/Y CTE `12 / 15 ppm/C` from `40 to 125 C`.
- The table lists flammability `V-0`.
- The table lists `Dk 4.6 @ 1 GHz` and `Dk 4.4 @ 10 GHz`, plus `Df 0.011 @ 1 GHz` and `Df 0.013 @ 10 GHz`, by `IEC 61189-2-721`, etched, `RC50%`.
- The table lists CTI `>=175 V`, dielectric breakdown `>=45 kV`, arc resistance `120 sec`, water absorption `0.11%`, and flexural strength `550 / 490 N/mm2`.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep the specimen note attached: typical value of specimen thickness is `1.6 mm`, `#7628 x 8`.
- Treat all values as typical reference values unless the source states a minimum or other bound.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not prove RoHS/WEEE finished-board compliance, OEM mandate satisfaction, proprietary chemistry, regional regulatory status, automotive qualification, or voltage-spacing suitability.
- It does not authorize cost, supplier-status, or HIL/APT capability claims.
