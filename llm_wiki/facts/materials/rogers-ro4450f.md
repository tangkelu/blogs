# Canonical Summary

> `RO4450F` is an official Rogers exact-product bondply with datasheet-backed electrical, thermal, and physical values that are reusable only when kept separate from Rogers RF core laminates and tied to the stated test conditions.

## Stable Facts

- Rogers publishes an official `RO4400 Series Bondply` datasheet that includes `RO4450F` as a named exact product.
- The datasheet identifies `RO4450F` as part of the `RO4400` bondply family based on `RO4000` series core materials.
- `Dielectric constant` is `3.52 ± 0.05` at `10 GHz` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.5` under `23 C @ 50% RH`.
- `Dissipation factor` is `0.004` at `10 GHz` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.5` under `23 C @ 50% RH`.
- `Volume resistivity` is `8.93 x 10^8 Mohm-cm` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.17.1` under `C-96/35/90`.
- `Surface resistivity` is `1.03 x 10^7 Mohm` in `X,Y` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.17.1` under `C-96/35/90`.
- `Electrical strength` is `1000 V/mil` in `Z` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.6.2` under `D-48/50`.
- `Td` is `390 C` by `IPC-TM-650 2.3.40` at `5% weight loss` after `2 hrs @ 105 C`.
- `Tg` is `>280 C` by `IPC-TM-650 2.4.24.5` with `TMA A`.
- `CTE-x` is `19 ppm/C`, `CTE-y` is `17 ppm/C`, and `CTE-z` is `50 ppm/C` from `-55 C to 288 C` by `IPC-TM-650 2.4.41`.
- `Thermal conductivity` is `0.65 W/(m.K)` in the `z` direction at `80 C` by `ASTM D5470`.
- `Copper peel strength after thermal stress` is `0.70 N/mm (4.0 lbs/in)` in `Z` with `35 um foil` after `10s @ 288 C` by `IPC-TM-650 2.4.8`.
- `Resin content` is `80`.
- `Flammability` is `UL 94 V-0`.
- `Moisture absorption` is `0.04%` by `IPC-TM-650 2.6.2.1` under `D24/23`.
- `Lead free process compatible` is listed as `Yes`.
- Rogers also positions `RO4450F` as the improved-lateral-flow member of the family and the first choice for difficult fill requirements.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep every value tied to the official `RO4400 Series Bondply` datasheet and its stated method, direction, and condition.
- Treat all listed values as typical datasheet values rather than universal guaranteed specification limits.
- Keep `RO4450F` separate from `RO4460G2`; do not collapse them into one average bondply row.
- Use this card when a draft needs a real Rogers bondply row inside hybrid `Rogers + FR-4` or multilayer RF evidence, not when it needs RF core-laminate values.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not let `RO4450F` values stand in for `RO4003C`, `RO4350B`, `RO4835`, or any other Rogers core laminate.
- It does not prove a complete hybrid stackup recipe, lamination window, board-level SI outcome, or plated-through-hole reliability result.
- It does not turn family marketing language such as `difficult fill requirements` into a generic manufacturability or capability claim.
- It does not prove inventory status, availability, or cost position.
