# Canonical Summary

> `IT-150DA` is now an exact-product ITEQ low-loss laminate anchor with official page-backed and datasheet-backed values strong enough for conservative high-Tg digital-material comparisons.

## Stable Facts

- ITEQ publishes an official product page for `IT-150DA`.
- ITEQ also publishes a public datasheet PDF for `IT-150DA`.
- The page identifies `IT-150DA` as a named high-Tg low-loss product for server/storage/switch, backplane, telecom, base station, and radio-frequency context.
- The page lists `Tg 180 C` by `DSC`.
- The page lists `T288 >30 min` with `1 oz Cu` by `TMA`.
- The page lists `Td 370 C` by `TGA 5% loss`.
- The page lists `CTE 45/250 ppm/C` as `a1/a2`.
- The page lists `CTE 2.6%` from `50 to 260 C` by `TMA`.
- The page lists `Dk 3.64` at `10 GHz` with `RC 50%` by `IPC TM-650 2.5.5.13`.
- The page lists `Df 0.0065` at `10 GHz` with `RC 50%` by `IPC TM-650 2.5.5.13`.
- The page lists `CTI 3 (175-249)` by `IEC 60112 / UL 746`.
- The datasheet also provides multi-frequency dielectric values, `T260 >60 min`, moisture absorption `0.10%`, peel strength `2.5-3.5 lb/in`, `UL94 V-0`, `MOT 130 C`, and in-plane `CTE 12/14 ppm/C` from `40 to 125 C`.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep all numeric values tied to the official `IT-150DA` page section and linked datasheet.
- Keep dielectric values tied to their stated frequency and resin-content conditions, especially `10 GHz` and `RC 50%`.
- Treat application wording as product positioning context, not as stackup, inventory, or fabricator-capability proof.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not make `IT-150DA` interchangeable with `IT-180A`, `IT-602G`, `IT-968`, Isola low-loss products, or Panasonic `MEGTRON` grades.
- It does not turn supplier application wording into guaranteed `25G/56G/112G` channel performance.
- It does not prove a specific layer-count capability, via architecture, or backplane qualification outcome.
