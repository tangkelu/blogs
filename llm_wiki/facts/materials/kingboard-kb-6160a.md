# Canonical Summary

> `KB-6160A` is an exact-product KBLaminates FR-4 laminate / prepreg system with an official PDF source. Its values must remain source-scoped and must not be inferred from or generalized to adjacent `KB-6160` suffixes.

## Stable Facts

- KBLaminates identifies `KB-6160A` laminate with `KB-6060A` prepreg.
- The official PDF lists `Tg 135 C`, Z-axis CTE total expansion `4.3%` from `50 to 260 C`, and `Td 305 C`.
- The PDF lists `Dk 4.35 @ 1 MHz` and `Dk 4.25 @ 1 GHz`, plus `Df 0.017 @ 1 MHz` and `Df 0.018 @ 1 GHz`.
- The PDF lists CTI `>=175 V`, moisture absorption `0.19%`, and a thickness list from `0.05 mm` to `3.20 mm`.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep all values tied to the official 2021 portal PDF unless a newer working official PDF supersedes it.
- Treat thickness-list information as material availability context only.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not prove HIL/APT stocking, capability, finished-board thickness availability, lead time, yield, or cost.
- It does not validate KB-6160, KB-6160F, KB-6160LC, or KB-6160LC(C).
