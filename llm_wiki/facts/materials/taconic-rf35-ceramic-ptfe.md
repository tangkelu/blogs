# Canonical Summary

> Taconic RF-30/RF-35 (ORCER family) are ceramic-filled PTFE laminates with Dk 3.50 ± 0.05 and Df 0.0018, offering notably lower loss than competitive materials.

## Stable Facts

### Electrical Properties (@ 10 GHz)
- **Dk**: 3.00 - 3.50 (3.50 ± 0.05 for RF-35)
- **Df**: 0.0018
- **Comparison**: Lower Df than Rogers RO4350B (0.0037)

### Mechanical/Thermal
- **CTE X/Y**: 14 ppm/°C
- **CTE Z**: 30 ppm/°C (matched to copper)
- **Thermal Conductivity**: 0.62 W/m·K

### Key Characteristics
- Ceramic-filled PTFE (ORCER family)
- Tight Dk tolerance: ±0.05
- Lead-free solder compatible
- Plasma desmear recommended for optimal via reliability
- RF-35A2 is most commonly specified variant

### Applications
- Sub-6 GHz 5G macro and small-cell antennas
- Power amplifier pallets
- GPS receivers
- WLAN enterprise access points
- General commercial RF

## Conditions And Methods

- Stocked at APTPCB: 10-60 mil thickness, 1/2 oz and 1 oz ED copper
- Other series: 5-10 working days from distributors

## Limits And Non-Claims

- Does not guarantee specific antenna performance
- Copper peel strength varies by surface treatment
- Via reliability depends on plasma parameters
