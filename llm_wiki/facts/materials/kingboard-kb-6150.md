# Canonical Summary

> `KB-6150` is an exact-product KBLaminates conventional normal-Tg FR-4 laminate with an official technical-information table, but its public PDF only authorizes source-scoped laminate values and does not prove lead-free assembly margin, board capability, cost position, or application qualification.

## Stable Facts

- KBLaminates identifies `KB-6150` as `Conventional FR-4 / Normal Tg`.
- The official table references `UL E123995` and states `IPC-4101E/21` is applicable.
- For the stated specimen scope, the table lists thermal stress `>=180 sec` at `Float 288 C / Unetched`, `Tg 132 C` by `IPC-TM-650 2.4.25 DSC`, and `Td 305 C` by `IPC-TM-650 2.4.24.6 TGA`.
- The table lists Z-axis CTE `Alpha 1 58 ppm/C` and `Alpha 2 286 ppm/C` by `IPC-TM-650 2.4.24`.
- The table lists flammability `V-0` by `UL94 E-24/125`.
- The table lists `Dk 4.6 @ 1 MHz` and `Dk 4.4 @ 1 GHz`, plus `Df 0.017 @ 1 MHz` and `Df 0.018 @ 1 GHz`, by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.9`, etched, `RC50%`.
- The table lists CTI `>=150 V`, dielectric breakdown `>=45 kV`, arc resistance `125 sec`, water absorption `0.17%`, and flexural strength `560 / 440 N/mm2`.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep the specimen note attached: typical value of specimen thickness is `1.6 mm`, `#7628 x 8`.
- Treat all values as typical reference values unless the source states a minimum or other bound.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not claim KB-6150 is lead-free qualified, high-reliability, or suitable for a specific layer count or reflow count.
- It does not authorize cost, stocking, lead-time, supplier-status, or HIL/APT capability claims.
- It does not prove finished-board compliance or application qualification.
