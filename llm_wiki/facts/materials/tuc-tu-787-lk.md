# Canonical Summary

> TU-787 LK is a TUC halogen-free low-Dk / low-Df laminate and TU-787P LK prepreg for mobile / telecom material contexts. Its values must stay tied to the stated RC, frequency, and method labels.

## Stable Facts

- TUC identifies TU-787 LK core and TU-787P LK prepreg as a halogen-free laminate and prepreg with datasheet code `DS2203002A`.
- TUC frames TU-787 LK around smart phone, telecom, office router, and mobile communication contexts.
- The sheet lists IPC-4101 type designations `/127`, `/128`, `/130`, UL file `E189572`, flammability `94V-0`, and maximum operating temperature `130 C`.
- The sheet lists Tg `160 C` by TMA and Td `380 C` by TGA.
- The same sheet lists X-axis CTE and Y-axis CTE `11~15 ppm/C`, Z-axis CTE alpha1 `40 ppm/C`, Z-axis CTE alpha2 `220 ppm/C`, and Z-axis expansion `2.7%` from `50 C to 260 C`.
- The same sheet lists thermal stress solder float at `288 C` as `>60 sec` and T-288 `>60 min`.
- At `RC75`, the sheet lists Dk `3.2 / 3.4` at `1 GHz` by `4291B / SPC`.
- At `RC75`, the sheet lists Dk `3.4` at `10 GHz` by `SPC`.
- At `RC75`, the sheet lists Df `0.005 / 0.007` at `1 GHz` by `4291B / SPC`.
- At `RC75`, the sheet lists Df `0.009` at `10 GHz` by `SPC`.
- The same sheet lists volume resistivity `>10^10 MOhm-cm`, surface resistivity `>10^8 MOhm`, peel strength `5~7 lb/in` for `1.0 oz Cu foil`, and water absorption `0.08%`.
- The sheet lists thickness availability from `0.002 in / 0.05 mm` to `0.006 in / 0.15 mm`, copper foil cladding from `1/3 oz` to `2 oz`, and glass styles including `1017`, `1027`, `1037`, `1067`, and `1078`.

## Conditions And Methods
