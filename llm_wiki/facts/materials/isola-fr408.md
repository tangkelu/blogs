# Canonical Summary

> `FR408` is an exact-product Isola laminate/prepreg anchor that can serve as a better-defined higher-performance `FR-4`-family example, as long as every reused property stays tied to the official datasheet and its stated conditions.

## Stable Facts

- Isola publishes an official exact-product datasheet for `FR408` laminate and prepreg.
- The datasheet is safe to use for `FR408` product identity and product-specific material context.
- The registered source summary explicitly treats `FR408` as a product-level `FR-4` anchor rather than a universal `FR-4` value set.
- Within the current corpus, `FR408` is already framed as one of the better-defined higher-performance `FR-4`-family examples.
- Any numeric property recovered from `FR408` must preserve the datasheet's test method, condition, and any `typical` versus guaranteed language.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep all `FR408` values attached to the official datasheet, not to generic `FR-4` wording.
- Preserve the exact property condition when reusing numbers, including frequency, test method, and any laminate/prepreg-specific scope.
- Treat the card as an exact-product reference row for `FR-4`-family writing, not as a family-wide default.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not make `FR408` interchangeable with `FR408HR`, `370HR`, `IS410`, or non-Isola `FR-4` systems.
- It does not claim one universal `FR-4` `Dk`, `Df`, `Tg`, `CTE`, or loss profile.
- It does not make capability, stack recipe, availability, stock, or procurement claims.
- It does not promote `FR408` from a product-specific anchor into proof for all `FR-4` designs.
