# Canonical Summary

> N4000-29 is an AGC lead-free high-Tg low-CTE multifunctional epoxy laminate and prepreg. It can support material-level high-temperature and hybrid-Meteorwave framing, but not finished-board reliability or supplier capability claims.

## Stable Facts

- AGC describes N4000-29 as an advanced lead-free, low-CTE, high-Tg multifunctional epoxy PCB material.
- The TDS frames applications around lead-free assembly structures, large-format backplanes, high-temperature underhood automotive, telecommunications infrastructure, and data storage.
- The TDS states N4000-29 is compatible with Meteorwave products for hybrid applications to reduce package cost; this is material-positioning language, not a build-qualification result.
- The TDS lists dielectric constant of `4.23 at 2 GHz` and `4.16 at 10 GHz` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.5`.
- The same TDS lists dissipation factor of `0.015 at 2.5 GHz` and `0.017 at 10 GHz`.
- The same TDS lists volume resistivity `8.10 x 10^7 MOhm-cm` at `C-96 / 35 / 90` and `1.90 x 10^8 MOhm-cm` at `E-24 / 125`.
- The same TDS lists surface resistivity `5.60 x 10^6 MOhm` at `C-96 / 35 / 90` and `1.80 x 10^7 MOhm` at `E-24 / 125`.
- The same TDS lists electric strength `4.2 x 10^4 V/mm` / `1100 V/mil`.
- The same TDS lists Tg `199 C` by DMA `Tan d Peak` and `185 C` by DSC.
- The same TDS lists degradation temperature `350 C` at `5%` weight loss, `T-260 > 60 minutes`, and `T-288 20 minutes`.
- The same TDS lists thermal conductivity `0.46 W/mK`.
- The same TDS lists X/Y CTE from `-40 C to +125 C` of `12 / 15 ppm/C`, Z-axis CTE Alpha 1 / Alpha 2 at `55% RC` of `55 / 265 ppm/C`, and Z-axis expansion at `43% RC` of `3.0%` from `50 C to 260 C`.
- The same TDS lists moisture absorption `0.15 wt.%`.
- The TDS states N4000-29 meets `UL 94V-0` and `IPC-4101 /98`, `/99`, `/126`, and `/129`, with UL file number `E36295`.
- The TDS states N4000-29 can be manufactured in laminate thickness from `2 mil / 0.05 mm` and up.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep the `2 GHz`, `2.5 GHz`, and `10 GHz` electrical values separate.
- Preserve `55% RC` for the Z-axis CTE row and `43% RC` for the Z-axis expansion row.
- Treat all listed values as typical datasheet values, not guaranteed specification limits.
- Do not generalize Meteorwave hybrid compatibility into a stackup recipe or production result.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not prove finished-board backplane, automotive, telecom, data-storage, via-to-via, BGA, or extreme-layer-count qualification.
- It does not prove multiple-reflow survival, repair-operation success, IST performance, CAF lifetime, or plated-through-hole reliability for a finished board.
- It does not prove APT, HIL, or any other supplier capability, certification, yield, lead time, stock, price, or qualification status.
- It does not provide a reusable lamination recipe for suppliers or designs.
