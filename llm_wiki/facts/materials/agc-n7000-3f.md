# Canonical Summary

> `N7000-3F` is an AGC product-grade toughened filled polyimide laminate and prepreg system with condition-bound datasheet values that must stay tied to the official test methods.

## Stable Facts

- AGC publishes an official datasheet for `N7000-3F`.
- The official source posture is strong enough to treat `N7000-3F` as an exact-product baseline material anchor.
- The datasheet identifies `N7000-3F` as a toughened filled polyimide laminate and prepreg system.
- `Dk` is `4.0` at `10 GHz` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.9` and is a typical value.
- `Df` is `0.014` at `10 GHz` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.5` and is a typical value.
- `Tg` is `260 C` by `DSC` and `IPC-TM-650 2.4.25c`.
- `Td` is `376 C` by `TGA` at `5% wt. loss` and `IPC-TM-650 2.4.24.6`.
- `T260` is `120+ min` by `IPC-TM-650 2.4.24.1`.
- `thermal conductivity` is `0.45 W/mK` by `ASTM E1461`.
- `peel strength` is `1.09 N/mm (6.2 lbf/inch)` after solder float with `1 oz (35 um) Cu` by `IPC-TM-650 2.4.8`.
- `CTE-x` is `14 ppm/C` and `CTE-y` is `16 ppm/C` from `-40 C to +125 C` by `IPC-TM-650 2.4.41`.
- `z-axis expansion` is `< 1.2%` from `63% RC` and `50 C to 260 C` by `IPC-TM-650 2.4.24`.
- `moisture absorption` is `0.35 wt.%` by `IPC-TM-650 2.6.2.1`.
- `flammability` is `UL 94 V-1`.
- The source layer also supports `IPC-4101E /40 /41 /42` slash-sheet framing.
- The datasheet supports guarded multilayer wording for fine-geometry multilayer constructions and heavy-copper polyimide multilayers.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep every numeric value tied to the official `N7000-3F` datasheet and the exact conditions shown there.
- Treat all listed values as typical datasheet values rather than guaranteed universal specification values.
- Treat `multilayer`, `polyimide`, and `fine geometry multilayer` wording as product context only, not as proof of a default stackup, lamination count, or fabricator capability.
- Recheck the official datasheet before reusing exact values in comparisons or selector tables.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not create a generic `20-layer` recipe or prove any specific multilayer build.
- It does not prove qualification, reliability, or manufacturability for a finished board design.
- It does not turn laminate/prepreg identity into a universal electrical value across all constructions.
- It does not replace stackup-specific SI, thermal, reliability, or fabrication validation.
- It does not promote `low Z-axis CTE` into an unstated numeric `cte_z`.
- It does not rename `moisture absorption` into a different test definition.
- It does not imply every dielectric row is identical across thickness or glass style.
