# Canonical Summary

> Current `llm_wiki` evidence supports exact ITEQ grades such as `IT-988G`, `IT-988GL`, `IT-988GSE`, and `IT-988GLSE`. It does not confirm `IT-988SE` as a reusable exact-product name, so old-blog `IT-988SE` references should be removed, replaced with a verified exact grade, or downgraded to an unresolved naming note.

## Stable Facts

- Existing ITEQ source records verify exact product rows for `IT-988G`, `IT-988GL`, `IT-988GSE`, and `IT-988GLSE`.
- Existing ITEQ fact cards keep those rows separate by exact grade name.
- The current corpus does not have an official source record, datasheet, or product-page section for exact name `IT-988SE`.
- The current ITEQ ladder card already rejects suffix inference across `IT-988G`, `IT-988GL`, `IT-988GSE`, and `IT-988GLSE`.

## Usage Rules

- Do not use old-blog `IT-988SE` as a synonym for `IT-988GSE`, `IT-988GLSE`, `IT-988G`, or `IT-988GL`.
- If a draft needs an ITEQ ultra-low-loss example, select a verified exact product and cite its own fact/source card.
- If the old text specifically says `IT-988SE`, treat that row as `needs_source` until an official exact-name source is recovered.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not claim that `IT-988SE` does not exist anywhere.
- It only states that the current reusable `llm_wiki` corpus has not verified that exact public product name.
- It does not authorize suffix-based performance inference, product substitution, procurement substitution, or material ranking.
