# Canonical Summary

> `KB-6168LE` is an exact-product KBLaminates high-Tg / low-CTE laminate / prepreg system with official material-property coverage. It does not by itself prove mission, aerospace, downhole, or lifetime reliability outcomes.

## Stable Facts

- KBLaminates identifies `KB-6168LE` laminate with `KB-6068LE` prepreg.
- The official table lists `Tg 190 C` by DSC, `Tg 200 C` by DMA, `Td 370 C`, and Z-axis expansion `2.1%` from `50 to 260 C`.
- The table lists `Dk 4.8 @ 1 MHz` and `Dk 4.6 @ 1 GHz`, plus `Df 0.015 @ 1 MHz` and `Df 0.016 @ 1 GHz`.
- The table lists CTI `>=300 V`.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep all values tied to the official KBLaminates PDF.
- Treat values as typical reference values unless the source states a minimum or other bound.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not prove aerospace, defense, downhole, automotive, IST-cycle, life-cycle, via-fatigue, high-aspect-ratio, hybrid-stackup, cost, lead-time, or HIL/APT capability claims.
