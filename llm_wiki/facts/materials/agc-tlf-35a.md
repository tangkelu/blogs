# Canonical Summary

> TLF-35A is an AGC Dk 3.5 RF laminate with Dk / Df published at `10 GHz`. PIMD, PTH quality, attenuation, and price/performance wording must remain manufacturer framing unless separately proven.

## Stable Facts

- AGC identifies the product name as `TLF-35A`.
- At `10 GHz`, the TDS lists dielectric constant `3.50 +/- 0.05` by modified `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.5.1`.
- At `10 GHz`, the TDS lists dissipation factor `0.0026` by modified `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.5.1`.
- The same TDS lists surface resistivity `3.0 x 10^9 Mohms` and volume resistivity `2.0 x 10^8 Mohms/cm` by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.17.1`.
- The same TDS lists thermal conductivity `0.37 W/m/K` by `IPC-TM-650 2.4.50`.
- The same TDS lists CTE from `50 C to 150 C` as X `9 ppm/C`, Y `12 ppm/C`, and Z `80 ppm/C` by `IPC-650 2.4.41`.
- The same TDS lists peel strength for `1 oz` copper as `1.8 N/mm (10 lb/linear in)` by `IPC-TM-650 2.4.8`.
- The same TDS lists flexural strength lengthwise / crosswise as `90 / 90 N/mm2` by `IPC-TM-650 2.4.4`.
- The same TDS lists flammability `V-0` by `UL-94` and water absorption `0.03%` by `IPC-TM-650 2.6.2.1`.
- The sheet lists typical thicknesses `0.030 in / 0.76 mm` and `0.060 in / 1.52 mm`, multiple panel sizes, copper-cladding designations, and manufacture in `0.030 in / 0.76 mm` increments.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep Dk / Df tied to `10 GHz` and modified `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.5.1`.
- Treat improved PIMD, improved PTH quality, attenuation, and price/performance wording as manufacturer positioning only.
- Treat copper-cladding and panel-size rows as AGC product context, not supplier stock or build capability.
- Treat all data as typical values, matching the source note.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not prove antenna, power-amplifier, LNA, repeater, passive-component, filter, coupler, PIMD, PTH, attenuation, or RF finished-board performance.
- It does not prove cost advantage, price/performance, production yield, supplier capability, or application readiness.
- It does not prove APT, HIL, or any other supplier certification, price, MOQ, stock, lead time, or production approval.
