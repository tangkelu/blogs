# Canonical Summary

> Isola coverage now matches the main Isola materials named by APT/HIL pages: standard/high-Tg FR-4, lower-loss FR-4, high-speed digital laminates, RF/microwave Astra, and ultra-low-loss Tachyon.

## Stable Facts

- Official Isola datasheet anchors are now registered for 370HR, FR408, FR408HR, I-Speed, I-Tera MT40, IS410, Astra MT77, and Tachyon 100G.
- APT's Isola page can be used for public service framing and application grouping.
- Product-level material values must stay attached to the specific Isola datasheet and test conditions.

## Conditions And Methods

- Use this card for Isola material selector drafts, FR-4 explainers, high-speed stackup topics, and RF/hybrid material planning.
- Do not collapse Isola products into a single "Isola value"; each product remains its own material system.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not claim all Isola products are stocked.
- It does not validate internal lead-time or inventory statements.
- It does not replace stackup-specific impedance and loss modeling.

## Open Questions

- Add official anchors for IS420, IS550H, IS680, and TerraGreen if future drafts use those names beyond passing mention.
