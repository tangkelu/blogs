# Canonical Summary

> Arlon CLTE-XT is a commercial microwave laminate with extremely low loss (Df 0.0012) and Z-axis CTE matched to copper for superior plated through-hole reliability in multilayer RF boards.

## Stable Facts

### Electrical Properties
- **Dk**: 2.94 - 3.00
- **Df**: 0.0012 (extremely low loss)
- **Phase Stability**: Stable over temperature

### Mechanical/Thermal
- **Z-axis CTE**: Matched to copper for PTH reliability
- **Base Chemistry**: PTFE / Ceramic / Woven Glass

### Key Characteristics
- Extremely low loss for commercial microwave applications
- Z-axis CTE matched to copper (critical for multilayer RF)
- Phase stable over temperature
- Requires plasma desmear for via metallization
- Low-PIM performance for cellular applications

### Applications
- Phased-array radar antennas
- Low-PIM cellular base station feeds
- Collision avoidance radar
- Space communications

## Conditions And Methods

- Stocked at APTPCB in common thicknesses
- Plasma desmear mandatory for via reliability
- Multilayer construction benefits from CTE matching

## Limits And Non-Claims

- PIM performance depends on fabrication quality
- Phase stability varies with temperature range
- Does not guarantee specific radar performance
