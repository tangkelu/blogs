# Canonical Summary

> `PI-515G` is an exact-product KBLaminates halogen-free high-Tg laminate / prepreg system with official material-property coverage. It must not be inferred from `PI-520G`, and it does not validate generic polyimide behavior.

## Stable Facts

- KBLaminates identifies `PI-515G` laminate with `PP-PI515G` prepreg and references `UL E115974`.
- The official table lists thermal stress `>=240 sec`, `Tg 162 C` by DSC, `Tg 172 C` by DMA, `T-260 >60 min`, `T-288 >60 min`, and `Td 373 C`.
- The table lists Z-axis CTE `Alpha 1 41 ppm/C`, `Alpha 2 235 ppm/C`, total expansion `2.6%` from `50 to 260 C`, and X/Y CTE `12 / 15 ppm/C`.
- The table lists flammability `V-0`.
- The table lists `Dk 4.6 @ 1 GHz` and `Dk 4.4 @ 10 GHz`, plus `Df 0.011 @ 1 GHz` and `Df 0.013 @ 10 GHz`.
- The table lists CTI `>=200 V`, dielectric breakdown `>=45 kV`, arc resistance `124 sec`, peel strength `1.60 N/mm`, flexural strength `620 / 550 N/mm2`, and water absorption `0.10%`.
- The table references `IPC-4101E/127/128` and specimen thickness `1.6 mm`, `#7628 x 8`.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep all values tied to the official KBLaminates PDF.
- Treat values as typical reference values unless the source states a minimum or other bound.
- Keep PI-515G separated from PI-520G.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not prove generic polyimide behavior, PI-520G substitution, highest-portfolio ranking, via-fatigue math, application qualification, process-temperature expectations, quote support, certificates, cost, lead time, or HIL/APT capability.
