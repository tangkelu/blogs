# Canonical Summary

> `KB-6169GT` is an exact-product KBLaminates high-Tg laminate / prepreg system with official material-property coverage. It does not by itself validate 56G/112G, PAM4, PCIe, DDR, Ethernet, or channel-budget claims.

## Stable Facts

- KBLaminates identifies `KB-6169GT` laminate with `KB-6069GT` prepreg.
- The official table lists `Tg 193 C` by DSC, `Tg 210 C` by DMA, `Td 395 C`, and Z-axis expansion `1.9%` from `50 to 260 C`.
- The table lists `Dk 4.6 @ 1 GHz` and `Dk 4.4 @ 10 GHz`, plus `Df 0.011 @ 1 GHz` and `Df 0.013 @ 10 GHz`.
- The table lists CTI `>=600 V`.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep all values tied to the official KBLaminates PDF.
- Treat values as typical reference values unless the source states a minimum or other bound.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not prove 56G, 112G, PAM4, PCIe, DDR, Ethernet, insertion-loss budget, Dk dispersion, trace-length limits, HVLP mandates, AI/radar suitability, cost, lead time, or HIL/APT capability.
