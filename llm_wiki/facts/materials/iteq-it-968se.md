# Canonical Summary

> `IT-968SE` is an exact-product ITEQ ultra-low-loss laminate anchor with official public values strong enough for conservative `56Gbps+`, `PAM-2/PAM-4`, and hybrid-RF-oriented material comparisons.

## Stable Facts

- ITEQ publishes an official exact-product page section for `IT-968SE`.
- ITEQ also publishes a public datasheet PDF for `IT-968SE`.
- The page describes `IT-968SE` as a halogen-free, low-`CTE`, high-`Tg`, ultra-low-loss material.
- The page lists `Tg 180 C` by `TMA`.
- The page lists `T288 >60 min` with `1 oz Cu` by `TMA`.
- The page lists `Td 400 C` by `TGA 5% loss`.
- The page lists `CTE 53/280 ppm/C` as `a1/a2`.
- The page lists `CTE 2.61%` from `50 to 260 C` by `TMA`.
- The page lists `Dk 2.86` at `10 GHz` with `RC 70%` by `IPC TM-650 2.5.5.13`.
- The page lists `Df 0.0028` at `10 GHz` with `RC 70%` by `IPC TM-650 2.5.5.13`.
- ITEQ also associates `IT-968SE` with `56Gbps+ per channel`, `PAM-2/PAM-4`, server/storage/switch, telecom/base-station, and hybrid `RF/microwave` context.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep all numeric values tied to the official `IT-968SE` page section and linked datasheet.
- Keep dielectric values tied to `10 GHz` and `RC 70%`.
- Treat `56Gbps+`, `PAM-2/PAM-4`, and hybrid `RF/microwave` wording as application context, not as board-level SI or manufacturability proof.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not make `IT-968SE` interchangeable with `IT-968`, `IT-968G`, `IT-968GSE`, or any `IT-988*` grade.
- It does not prove a complete channel budget, insertion-loss target, or signal-integrity result on a finished board.
- It does not turn modified `FR-4` process compatibility into a generic fabrication guarantee.
