# Canonical Summary

> `IT-180A` is now an exact-product ITEQ high-Tg FR-4 anchor with official page-backed baseline values for mainstream multilayer and `HDI`-adjacent material ladders.

## Stable Facts

- ITEQ publishes an official exact-product page section for `IT-180A`.
- The official page positions `IT-180A` as a high-Tg multifunctional filled epoxy with high thermal reliability and `CAF` resistance.
- The page lists `Tg 175 C` by `DSC`.
- The page lists `T288 20 min` with `1 oz Cu` by `TMA`.
- The page lists `Td 345 C` by `TGA 5% loss`.
- The page lists `CTE 45/210 ppm/C` as `a1/a2`.
- The page lists `CTE 2.7%` from `50 to 260 C` by `TMA`.
- The page lists `Dk 4.1` at `10 GHz` with `RC 50%` by `IPC TM-650 2.5.5.13`.
- The page lists `Df 0.017` at `10 GHz` with `RC 50%` by `IPC TM-650 2.5.5.13`.
- The page lists `CTI 3 (175-249)` by `IEC 60112 / UL 746`.
- ITEQ also associates `IT-180A` with multilayer, `HDI`, backplane, server/networking, telecom, and heavy-copper application context.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep all numeric values tied to the official `IT-180A` page section and its stated methods.
- Keep dielectric values tied to `10 GHz` and `RC 50%`.
- Treat application wording and `260 C lead free assembly` wording as product positioning context, not as stackup or fabricator-capability proof.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not make `IT-180A` interchangeable with all ITEQ, Isola, Shengyi, or Panasonic FR-4 systems.
- It does not prove a specific layer-count capability, via architecture, or backplane qualification result.
- It does not turn product positioning into guaranteed `HDI`, heavy-copper, or telecom board performance.
