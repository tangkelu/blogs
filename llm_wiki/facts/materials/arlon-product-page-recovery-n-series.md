# Canonical Summary

> Arlon N-series official product pages can now anchor product identity for several site-relevant grades, but product parameters still require current datasheets or pre-publish refresh.

## Stable Facts

- APT's Arlon page makes Arlon a site-mentioned material family requiring baseline wiki coverage.
- Official Arlon product pages were verified for 33N, 35N, 37N, 45N, 47N, 84N, and 85N.
- Official Arlon application pages were verified for controlled-flow prepreg and heavy-copper layer contexts.
- These records improve source discovery and product identity coverage for Arlon polyimide, epoxy, and prepreg discussions.

## Conditions And Methods

- Use these product pages to anchor Arlon product identity in evidence packs.
- Use application pages only for application/source-discovery framing.
- Attach current product datasheets before publishing Dk, Df, Tg, Td, CTE, moisture absorption, resin flow, copper, thickness, or press-cycle values.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not make any Arlon grade parameter-ready.
- It does not validate stocking, lead time, substitutions, or customer design suitability.
