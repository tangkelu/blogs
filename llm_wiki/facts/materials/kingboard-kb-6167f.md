# Canonical Summary

> `KB-6167F` is an exact-product KBLaminates high-Tg / low-CTE FR-4 laminate and prepreg system with an official technical-information table.

## Stable Facts

- KBLaminates identifies `KB-6167F` laminate with `KB-6067F` prepreg and references `UL E123995`.
- The official table references `IPC-4101E/99/101/126`.
- For the stated specimen scope, the table lists thermal stress `>=240 sec` at `Float 288 C / Unetched`, `Tg 175 C` by DSC, `Tg 185 C` by DMA, `T-260 >60 min`, `T-288 >35 min`, and `Td 356 C`.
- The table lists Z-axis CTE `Alpha 1 40 ppm/C`, `Alpha 2 230 ppm/C`, total expansion `2.6%` from `50 to 260 C`, and X/Y CTE `12 / 15 ppm/C` from `40 to 125 C`.
- The table lists flammability `V-0`.
- The table lists `Dk 4.8 @ 1 MHz` and `Dk 4.6 @ 1 GHz`, plus `Df 0.015 @ 1 MHz` and `Df 0.016 @ 1 GHz`, by `IPC-TM-650 2.5.5.9`, etched, `RC50%`.
- The table lists CTI `>=175 V`, dielectric breakdown `>=45 kV`, arc resistance `129 sec`, water absorption `0.09%`, and flexural strength `550 / 485 N/mm2`.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep the specimen note attached: typical value of specimen thickness is `1.6 mm`, `#7628 x 8`.
- Treat all values as typical reference values unless the source states a minimum or other bound.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not prove OEM qualification, AEC readiness, PPAP, automotive cycle life, 30-layer capability, traceability, outgoing test scope, pricing, lead time, or HIL/APT capability.
