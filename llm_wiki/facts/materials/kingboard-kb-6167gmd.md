# Canonical Summary

> `KB-6167GMD` is an exact-product KBLaminates halogen-free low-loss laminate / prepreg system with official material-property coverage. It does not by itself validate PCIe, DDR, USB, Ethernet, or channel-speed claims.

## Stable Facts

- KBLaminates identifies `KB-6167GMD` laminate with `KB-6067GMD` prepreg.
- The official table lists `Tg 178 C` by DSC, `Tg 190 C` by DMA, `Td 387 C`, and Z-axis expansion `2.1%` from `50 to 260 C`.
- The table lists `Dk 4.1 @ 1 GHz` and `Dk 4.0 @ 10 GHz`, plus `Df 0.008 @ 1 GHz` and `Df 0.009 @ 10 GHz`.
- The table lists CTI `>=175 V`.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep all values tied to the official KBLaminates PDF.
- Treat values as typical reference values unless the source states a minimum or other bound.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not prove PCIe, DDR, USB, Ethernet, 10 Gbps, insertion-loss, eye-margin, BER, channel compliance, automotive compute suitability, cost, lead time, or HIL/APT capability.
