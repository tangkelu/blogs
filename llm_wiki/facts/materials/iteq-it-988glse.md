# Canonical Summary

> `IT-988GLSE` is an exact-product ITEQ ultra-low-loss laminate anchor with official public values strong enough for conservative `100G / 400G` and backplane material comparisons.

## Stable Facts

- ITEQ publishes an official exact-product page section for `IT-988GLSE`.
- The page describes `IT-988GLSE` as an advanced low-`CTE`, high-`Tg`, ultra-low-loss material.
- The page lists `Tg 180 C` by `TMA`.
- The page lists `T288 >60 min` with `1 oz Cu` by `TMA`.
- The page lists `Td 400 C` by `TGA 5% loss`.
- The page lists `CTE 35/190 ppm/C` as `a1/a2`.
- The page lists `CTE 1.7%` from `50 to 260 C` by `TMA`.
- The page lists `Dk 3.12` at `10 GHz` with `RC 70%` by `IPC TM-650 2.5.5.13`.
- The page lists `Df 0.0025` at `10 GHz` with `RC 70%` by `IPC TM-650 2.5.5.13`.
- ITEQ also associates `IT-988GLSE` with `100G/400G switch`, server/storage/switch, backplane, telecom, and base-station context.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep all numeric values tied to the official `IT-988GLSE` page section and its stated methods.
- Keep dielectric values tied to `10 GHz` and `RC 70%`.
- Treat `100G/400G switch` wording as application context, not as a board-level SI guarantee.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not prove a complete channel budget, insertion-loss target, or signal-integrity result on a finished board.
- It does not make `IT-988GLSE` interchangeable with `IT-988GSE`, `IT-988GL`, `IT-988G`, or other ultra-low-loss laminates.
- It does not turn modified `FR-4` process compatibility into a generic fabrication guarantee.
