# Canonical Summary

> `KB-6160LC` is an exact-product KBLaminates low-CTE FR-4 laminate with an official technical-information PDF. Keep it separated from `KB-6160LC(C)`.

## Stable Facts

- KBLaminates identifies `KB-6160LC` in an official PDF.
- The official PDF lists `Tg 135 C`, Z-axis CTE `Alpha 1 45 ppm/C`, `Alpha 2 240 ppm/C`, total expansion `3.6%` from `50 to 260 C`, `T-260 >10 min`, and `Td 350 C`.
- The PDF lists `Dk 4.8 @ 1 MHz` and `Dk 4.6 @ 1 GHz`, plus `Df 0.017 @ 1 MHz` and `Df 0.018 @ 1 GHz`.
- The PDF lists CTI `>=175 V` and water absorption `0.10%`.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep all values tied to the official KBLaminates PDF.
- Treat values as typical reference values unless the source states a minimum or other bound.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not prove drill-cost savings, impedance shift, layer-count suitability, HIL/APT capability, cost, lead time, or yield.
- It does not validate KB-6160LC(C) or other KB-6160-family suffixes.
