# Canonical Summary

> Rogers material selection is not a single ranking. The practical choice depends on whether the priority is FR-4-like processing, low loss, very high-Dk miniaturization, thermal robustness, or a thermally stronger microwave thermoset option.

## Stable Facts

- RO4003C and RO4350B are the Rogers options in this registry that are explicitly framed around epoxy/glass-style processing.
- RO3003 and RT/duroid 5880 are the lower-loss options in this registry, with RT/duroid 5880 carrying the lowest published Df among the current cards.
- RO3006 and RO3010 are the higher-Dk compactness-oriented options in the current registry.
- RO3035 occupies a middle position with lower loss than RO4000 thermoset options and a moderate Dk higher than RO3003.
- The TMM family provides a thermoset microwave path when dimensional stability, plated-through-hole behavior, and in some grades higher thermal conductivity are important.
- RO4835 adds oxidation-resistance framing inside the RO4000 family.

## Conditions And Methods

- This selector card is an engineering summary inferred from official product properties and positioning.
- Treat it as a selection aid, not as a complete signoff rule.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card is not a substitute for path-loss simulation, stackup review, transition modeling, or manufacturing review.
- It does not include Taconic, Isola, Panasonic, Ventec, AGC, or non-Rogers alternatives.
