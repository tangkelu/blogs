# Canonical Summary

> RO4835 is a Rogers RO4000-series hydrocarbon/ceramic laminate positioned as a low-loss RF material with stronger oxidation resistance than standard RO4000 options, making it relevant for harsher thermal environments and lead-free assembly contexts.

## Stable Facts

- Rogers states RO4835 laminate provides oxidation resistance.
- The product page lists `Dk 3.48 +/- 0.05` and `Df 0.0037 at 10 GHz`.
- The same page lists thermal conductivity `0.66 W/m/K`, water absorption `0.05`, and lead-free process compatibility `Yes`.
- Rogers positions RO4835 for applications requiring enhanced oxidation resistance and higher reliability in thermally stressful conditions.
