# Canonical Summary

> `PI-520G` is an exact-product KBLaminates halogen-free / high-Tg / low-CTE laminate and prepreg system with an official technical-information table. This card does not validate `PI-515G` or generic polyimide claims.

## Stable Facts

- KBLaminates identifies `PI-520G` laminate with `PP-PI520G` prepreg and references `UL E115974`.
- The official table references `IPC-4101E/127/128/130`.
- For the stated specimen scope, the table lists thermal stress `>=240 sec` at `Float 288 C / Unetched`, `Tg 204 C` by DSC, `Tg 215 C` by DMA, `T-260 >60 min`, `T-288 >60 min`, and `Td 412 C`.
- The table lists Z-axis CTE `Alpha 1 36 ppm/C`, `Alpha 2 210 ppm/C`, total expansion `1.9%` from `50 to 260 C`, and X/Y CTE `12 / 15 ppm/C` from `40 to 125 C`.
- The table lists flammability `V-0`.
- The table lists `Dk 4.6 @ 1 GHz` and `Dk 4.5 @ 10 GHz`, plus `Df 0.011 @ 1 GHz` and `Df 0.013 @ 10 GHz`, by `IEC 61189-2-721`, etched, `RC50%`.
- The table lists CTI `>=200 V`, dielectric breakdown `>=45 kV`, arc resistance `124 sec`, water absorption `0.10%`, and flexural strength `600 / 530 N/mm2`.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep the specimen note attached: typical value of specimen thickness is `1.6 mm`, `#7628 x 8`.
- Treat all values as typical reference values unless the source states a minimum or other bound.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not prove `PI-515G` values, generic polyimide behavior, highest-portfolio ranking, via-fatigue math, application qualification, process-temperature expectations, quote support, certificates, or HIL/APT capability.
