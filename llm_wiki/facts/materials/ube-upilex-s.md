# Canonical Summary

> `UPILEX-S` is an exact-product UBE polyimide film anchor. It can support source-scoped PI film discussion and grade-specific material values, but it does not by itself authorize flex PCB bend-life, bend-radius, rigid-flex stackup, or finished-board reliability claims.

## Stable Facts

- UBE publishes official `UPILEX` grade details and UPILEX family pages.
- UBE identifies `UPILEX-S` as the standard grade of UBE polyimide film.
- UBE describes `UPILEX-S` as having better surface smoothness and higher chemical resistance, stiffness, heat resistance, and low outgassing compared with other polyimide films in its page wording.
- UBE lists `UPILEX-S` grade thickness rows including `12.5 um`, `25 um`, `50 um`, `75 um`, and `125 um`.
- UBE lists mechanical-property rows for `UPILEX-25S`, `UPILEX-50S`, `UPILEX-75S`, and `UPILEX-125S`.
- UBE lists `UPILEX-25S` tensile strength values including `740 MPa` at `-269 C`, `650 MPa` at `-196 C`, `520 MPa` at `25 C`, and `290 MPa` at `300 C` by `ASTM D882`.
- UBE lists `UPILEX-25S` tensile modulus values including `9.1 GPa` at `25 C` and `3.7 GPa` at `300 C` by `ASTM D882`.
- UBE lists `UPILEX-S` density as `1.47 x 10^3 kg/m3` where shown by `ASTM D1505`.
- UBE lists `UPILEX-S` thermal linear expansion coefficient values from `12 ppm/C` to `22 ppm/C` across shown grades over `50-200 C` by fine linear dilatometer at `5 C/min`.
- UBE lists `UPILEX-S` heat shrinkage values from `0.05%` to `0.01%` across shown grades under `200 C, 2 h` by `ASTM D1204`.
- UBE lists `UPILEX-S` thermal conductivity as `0.29 W/(m K)` in the thickness direction by laser flash method.
- UBE states that `UPILEX-S` is ideal for use in `FPC` and `TAB-tape` substrates composed of minute circuits in the thermal-property context.

## Conditions And Methods

- Keep all values tied to UBE's `UPILEX-S` grade-details page.
- Preserve grade, film thickness, temperature, condition, and test method whenever using property rows.
- Use this card when a draft needs a verified `UPILEX-S` product example rather than generic `UPILEX`, `polyimide`, or `PI` wording.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not make `UPILEX-S` interchangeable with all `UPILEX` grades, all polyimide films, `Kapton HN`, `LCP`, Panasonic `R-F705S`, Arlon `85NT`, or rigid-flex laminate systems.
- It does not provide flex PCB bend-radius, bend-cycle, copper-type, coverlay, adhesive, or stackup rules.
- It does not prove HIL/APT material availability, production use, supplier approval, IPC-6013 compliance, Class 3 status, lot acceptance, cost, lead time, or yield.
- It does not turn film-level thermal, tensile, or dimensional data into finished-board reliability proof.
