# Canonical Summary

> VT-464GS is a Ventec halogen-free high-Tg laminate / prepreg positioned for IC-packaging and high-speed / high-frequency contexts. Its electrical values are tied to `RC 70%` and `2 GHz` cavity-resonator conditions.

## Stable Facts

- Ventec identifies VT-464GS as a laminate / prepreg with UL approval `E214381`, version `Preliminary 1`, IPC-4101E `/130`.
- Ventec frames VT-464GS around handheld equipment, high-frequency and high-speed use, satellite communication, navigation, GPS, LTE, CSP, flip-chip package, SIP, BGA, and PGA contexts.
- The sheet lists Tg `260 C` by TMA / `IPC-TM-650 2.4.24` and Tg `300 C` by DMA / `IPC-TM-650 2.4.24.4`.
- The same sheet lists Td `450 C` by `ASTM D3850`, T260 `>60 min`, T288 `>60 min`, and thermal stress at `288 C` as `>600 s`.
- The same sheet lists Z-axis CTE before Tg `20 ppm/C`, total expansion from `50 C to 260 C` as `0.8%`, X-Y CTE `6~8 ppm/C`, and MOT `130 C`.
- At `RC 70%`, the sheet lists Dk `3.5` at `2 GHz` by cavity resonator.
- At `RC 70%`, the sheet lists Df `0.005` at `2 GHz` by cavity resonator.
- The same sheet lists moisture absorption `0.15%`, CTI `Grade 3 (175~250)`, arc resistance `195 s`, and flammability `V-0`.
- The sheet lists core thickness availability from `0.002 in / 0.05 mm` to `0.031 in / 0.8 mm`, copper foil availability from `1/7 oz` to `2 oz`, and prepreg thickness from `0.002 mm` to `0.1 mm`.
