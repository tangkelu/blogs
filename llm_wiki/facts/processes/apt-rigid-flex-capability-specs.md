# Canonical Summary

> APT Rigid-Flex capabilities: 1-32 layers, 0.025mm ultra-thin flex core, 2.5/2.5 mil trace, 0.075mm microvia, 10 oz rigid copper.

## Stable Facts

### Layer Count
- Up to 32 layers total

### Flex Core
- **Thickness**: 0.025 mm (ultra-thin)
- Enables high-density flexible interconnects

### Trace/Space
- **Minimum**: 2.5/2.5 mil (0.064/0.064 mm)

### Microvia
- **Diameter**: 0.075 mm (3 mil)
- HDI capable

### Copper Weight
- **Rigid sections**: Up to 10 oz

### Applications
- Automotive systems
- Industrial equipment
- Medical devices
- Aerospace
- High-end consumer electronics

### Features
- HDI microvias for dense routing
- Fine-pitch BGA support
- Comprehensive engineering/DFM guidance
- Seamless flexible-to-rigid transitions

## Conditions And Methods

- Sequential lamination for rigid-flex integration
- Controlled impedance for high-speed signals crossing flex/rigid boundaries
- Reliability testing for dynamic flex areas

## Limits And Non-Claims

- Flex layer count limited by overall thickness
- Dynamic flex life depends on design and materials
- Stiffener selection critical for mechanical stability
