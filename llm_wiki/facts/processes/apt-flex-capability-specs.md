# Canonical Summary

> APT Flex capabilities: 1–16 layers, polyimide/LCP materials, down to 0.05 mm thickness, 4/4 mil trace/space.

## Stable Facts

### Flex Types
- **Configurations**: Single-sided, double-sided, multi-layer
- **Max layers**: 1–16

### Materials
- **Primary**: Polyimide (PI), LCP (Liquid Crystal Polymer)
- **Options**: PET, PEN, FR-4 base films

### Dimensions
- **Min thickness**: 0.05 mm
- **Max board size**: 20.5″ × 13″ (panelized)

### Trace/Space
- **Min**: 4/4 mil

### Copper
- **Weight**: 1/3–2 oz

### Lead Times
- **DFM review**: <24 hours
- **Prototype**: 24–48 hours

### Applications
- Consumer devices
- Wearable electronics
- Automotive modules
- Medical equipment

## Conditions And Methods

- Bend-radius optimization for dynamic flex applications
- Material selection based on flex cycles and environment

## Limits And Non-Claims

- Minimum bend radius depends on layer count and material
- Dynamic flex life varies by design and material
- Max board size for panelized production
