# Canonical Summary

> APT MCPCB capabilities: Al/Cu/Fe base, 1-4 layers, 43.3×19 inch, 4/4 mil trace, impedance ±5%, 24-48h prototype.

## Stable Facts

### Metal Base Options
- **Aluminum (Al)**
- **Copper (Cu)**
- **Iron (Fe)**

### Board Dimensions
- **Max size**: 43.3″ × 19″

### Layer Count
- 1–4 layers

### Copper Weight
- 0.5–4 oz

### Trace/Space
- **Min**: 4/4 mil (with 0.5 oz copper)

### Surface Finishes
- HASL
- ENIG
- OSP
- Immersion Silver (Ag)

### Quality Standards
- IPC Class 2/3

### Impedance Control
- ±5%

### Lead Times
- **DFM review**: <24 hours
- **Prototype**: 24–48 hours

### Applications
- LED lighting
- Power electronics
- Automotive
- Any application requiring superior thermal conductivity and heat dissipation

## Conditions And Methods

- Thermal dielectric selection based on power density
- Board thickness optimized for thermal performance

## Limits And Non-Claims

- Max 4 layers (limited by metal core)
- Large boards may have bow/warp constraints
- Does not guarantee specific thermal performance without simulation
