# Canonical Summary

> APT Rigid capabilities: 1-64 layers, 2/2 mil trace/space, 610×1100mm board, up to 20 oz copper, 0.075mm laser drill.

## Stable Facts

### Layer Count
- Up to 64 layers

### Board Dimensions
- **Size range**: 10×10 mm to 610×1100 mm

### Trace/Space
- **Minimum**: 2/2 mil (0.05/0.05 mm)

### Copper Weight
- **Maximum**: Up to 20 oz

### Drilling
- **Mechanical**: 0.15 mm (6 mil)
- **Laser**: 0.075 mm (3 mil)

### Applications
- Industrial electronics
- Automotive systems
- Telecom infrastructure
- Medical devices
- High-speed digital

### Capabilities
- HDI structures
- Heavy copper (power applications)
- Fine-pitch BGAs
- Impedance control
- Complex stack-ups

### Materials
- FR-4 (standard and high-Tg)
- Low-loss RF materials
- Hybrid material stacks

### Support
- Full DFM (Design for Manufacturability) support
- Stack-up optimization
- Signal integrity consulting

## Conditions And Methods

- Advanced lamination for high layer counts
- Controlled impedance modeling
- AOI and electrical testing

## Limits And Non-Claims

- Fine-line capability depends on copper weight
- Heavy copper limits achievable trace/space
- Large boards may have registration challenges
