# Canonical Summary

> PSA and stiffener bonding should be written with method-scoped examples: adhesive wet-out / dwell on the PSA side, and flatness / warpage / connector-fit on the stiffener side.

## Stable Facts

- 3M 467MP is a PSA transfer tape whose bond development improves after dwell / natural ageing.
- Molex FPC connector application specs recommend backside stiffener or film to prevent deformation.
- Molex links board flatness, connector-edge warpage, and routing stress to connector reliability.

## Conditions And Methods

- Use this card when a blog needs to cite PSA wet-out, dwell, connector fit, or stiffener placement.
- Keep adhesive timing and connector fit in the same review chain.
- Use connector-spec-scoped examples for warpage / thickness only.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not authorize a universal stiffener thickness table.
- It does not turn PSA into a thermoset lamination claim.
- It does not prove final connector fit without the connector's own application spec.
