# Canonical Summary

> Official IPC and vendor sources support method and category boundaries for SIR, dielectric / insulation test families, conformal coatings, gels, encapsulants, and peelable coating masks. They do not prove waterproof PCB performance, IP ratings, high-voltage safety, service life, zero-residue masking, or universal process windows.

## Stable Facts

- IPC TM-650 `2.6.3.7` supports Surface Insulation Resistance (`SIR`) method vocabulary involving moisture, bias, leakage current, and electrochemical degradation effects from residues.
- The IPC TM-650 public index supports discovery vocabulary for dielectric breakdown, dielectric withstand, volume / surface resistance, moisture / insulation resistance, and conformal-coating insulation method families at metadata level.
- IPC-CC-830C public metadata anchors conformal coating as an electrical insulating compound topic for printed wiring assemblies.
- Dow's official category page separates conformal coatings, gels, and encapsulants as protection families.
- MacDermid Alpha / Electrolube's peelable coating-mask page supports peelable mask as a temporary masking material in conformal-coating / wave-soldering process context.

## Conditions And Methods

- Use this card when drafts discuss PCB insulation, SIR, waterproof PCB, conformal coating, potting, encapsulation, gel, peelable mask, coating keepout, or process protection.
- Reframe `waterproof PCB` language into assembly / enclosure / protection-family language unless an actual IP-rating or product-level qualification record is attached.
- Attach exact standards, product datasheets, test methods, customer specs, or dated process records before making numeric or qualification claims.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- This card does not support IP54 / IP67 / IP68 claims for a PCB or PCBA.
- It does not support dielectric-strength tables, creepage / clearance compliance, high-voltage certification, CTI thresholds, or universal safety qualification.
- It does not provide coating thickness, cure schedule, masking dimensions, peel-tab rules, removal guarantees, immersion time, salt-fog duration, lifetime, yield, or field-failure reduction.
- It does not prove supplier coating, potting, or peelable-mask process capability.
