# Canonical Summary

> For 5G PCB and RF content, the 3GPP 38-series public index is the right place to identify relevant NR specifications, and the TS 38.104 archive is the right place to anchor revision snapshots with dates.

## Stable Facts

- The 38-series public index lists NR specifications including TS 38.104 for base-station radio transmission and reception.
- The 38-series also includes FR1- and FR2-related UE and conformance specifications, so 5G PCB content should not collapse all 5G references into a single document.
- The TS 38.104 archive exposes dated revision packages rather than a timeless undifferentiated 'latest' file.

## Conditions And Methods

- Use the 38-series page to identify which specs are relevant to a topic.
- Use the archive path to record the exact snapshot used in a dated evidence pack.

## Limits And Non-Claims

- The public index does not replace reading the actual specification package.
- A wiki page should not claim a current latest revision without checking the archive on the publication date.
