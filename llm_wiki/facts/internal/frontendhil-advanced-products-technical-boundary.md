# Canonical Summary

> HILPCB advanced product portfolio spans 6 technology categories: (1) HDI PCB with 50-75μm laser microvias, VIPPO, and any-layer stackups for 10-56 Gbps; (2) Rigid-Flex IPC-6013 Class 3 with 3D integration and dynamic bend; (3) High-Frequency RF/mmWave with Df ≤0.0009, VNA to 40-67 GHz; (4) Rogers PCB with RO4350B/RO4003C/RT-duroid and hybrid stackups; (5) Ceramic PCB with AlN/Al2O3, DBC/DPC, 170-190 W/m·K thermal conductivity; (6) Turnkey Assembly with ±8-25μm placement, 3D SPI/AOI/X-ray, ICT/FCT, MES traceability.
