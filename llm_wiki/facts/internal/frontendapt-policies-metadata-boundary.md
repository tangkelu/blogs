# Canonical Summary

> APTPCB maintains four core policy documents covering privacy practices, service terms, quality management (ISO 9001:2015), and environmental management (ISO 14001:2015). These provide official policy boundaries but require currency verification before external use as all carry 2010 effective dates.

## Stable Facts

### Policy Coverage

| Policy Domain | Key Elements | Management System |
|---------------|--------------|-------------------|
| **Privacy** | Contact info, technical data, project files collection; GDPR/CCPA-ready framework | Data protection practices |
| **Terms of Service** | PCB manufacturing scope, SMT/through-hole assembly, DFM consultation, order acceptance process | Service delivery framework |
| **Quality** | Customer satisfaction, manufacturing excellence, continuous improvement objectives | ISO 9001:2015 |
| **Environmental** | Pollution prevention, resource conservation, waste minimization | ISO 14001:2015 |

### Service Scope Taxonomy (from Terms)

**PCB Manufacturing:**
- Single-layer, double-layer, multilayer PCB production
- Flexible, rigid-flex, specialty PCB manufacturing
- Prototype and small-batch production
- Quality testing and inspection

**PCB Assembly:**
- Surface Mount Technology (SMT) assembly
- Through-hole component assembly
- Mixed technology (SMT + Through-hole)
- Box build and system integration

**Support Services:**
- Design for Manufacturing (DFM) consultation
- Component sourcing and procurement
- Technical support and engineering assistance

### Data Collection Categories (from Privacy)

- **Contact Information:** Name, email, phone, address
- **Business Information:** Company, job title, industry
- **Technical Information:** IP address, browser, device identifiers, cookies
- **Project Information:** Gerber files, CAD files, specifications, quality data
