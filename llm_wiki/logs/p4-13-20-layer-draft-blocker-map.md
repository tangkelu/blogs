## Control Outcome

The draft remains blocked by three claim-cluster failures:

1. Geometry/capability collapse: public any-layer, build-up, and material-form sources are being rewritten into feature-size, registration, stack-recipe, and manufacturability proof.
2. HIL-specific proof leakage: supplier capability, production readiness, lead time, and compliance claims are presented without a separate dated evidence layer.
3. Reliability/process numeric leakage: method names and process-sensitivity context are being rewritten into thresholds, control-plan numerics, and qualification claims.

Release condition for this phase: the draft must be reduced to qualitative architecture, material-context, and workflow-level wording that stays inside the cited boundary cards above.
