## Current Blocker Shape

The current blocker shape for `registration tolerance` is:

1. the parameter is often implied indirectly through high-layer density, lamination sensitivity, or alignment difficulty wording rather than published as a directly authorized reusable field
2. inspection and workflow pages often discuss dimensional control without exposing reusable registration-tolerance numerics
3. standards-threshold or standards-family visibility can be mistaken for authority even when no reusable registration value is actually published
4. supplier marketing and process-discipline summaries can be overread as proof of transferable tolerance capability

## Acceptable Authority Requirement

`Registration tolerance` should be considered for reusable numeric handling only if all of the following are true:

1. a primary source exposes registration tolerance directly as a numeric parameter
2. the source has explicit scope and condition
3. the source is stronger than inspection vocabulary, workflow naming, or general dimensional-control posture
4. the source makes clear whether the value is public-general, process-bound, product-bound, supplier-scoped, or date-bound

Additional rule:

- inspection or workflow pages do not by themselves qualify as reusable numeric authority
- standards-threshold pages, clause-family summaries, and hierarchy pages do not by themselves qualify as reusable numeric authority
- supplier marketing or process-discipline summaries do not qualify as shared reusable numeric authority
