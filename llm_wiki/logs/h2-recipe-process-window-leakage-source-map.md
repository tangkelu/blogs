## Why Current Sources Do Not Authorize Reusable Recipe Or Process-Window Numerics

Current sources are mixed across:

- internal process prose
- heavy-copper special-process framing
- advanced-route examples
- supplier process guides
- application-note construction examples

That means the current corpus can support:

- process-family vocabulary
- planning posture
- recipe/process-window leakage control

It cannot support:

- reusable bake or cure windows
- reusable pressure or dwell ranges
- reusable route-sequence recipes
- transferable heavy-copper process defaults

## Candidate Split Families For Future Work

If later work continues here, claims must first be split into:

1. `process recipe example`
2. `process-window or timing example`
3. `supplier or application-note process context`
4. `adjacent true capability family`

Only a very narrow future family with a real `Tier 1 internal dated capability record` could ever attempt numeric recovery.

## What Is Still Missing

The missing item is not more process prose. The missing item is clean containment plus governed numeric authority for any very narrow future child claim.

Still missing:

- explicit separation between recipe examples and maintained production rules
- explicit separation between supplier process context and current shop authority
- explicit separation between process-window leakage and adjacent true capability families
- a registered `Tier 1 internal dated capability record` for any future narrow process-window claim
- usable date, owner, scope, and refresh posture for any future narrow record
- evidence that a cited number is maintained capability rather than recipe residue from one route or process guide

Without those items, `recipe_process_window_leakage` remains a containment area, not a reusable H2 numeric bucket.

## Current Ingestion Stop/Go Posture

- `keep`: process-family vocabulary and non-claim boundaries in the correct layer
- `downgrade`: workflow framing and route context without numeric promotion
- `hold`: recipe/process-window leakage containment
- `delete`: recipe examples or process-window claims rewritten as transferable factory defaults
