# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- Electrical, thermal, mechanical, chemical, physical, thickness, and sheet-size data for `RF-35TC`
- Manufacturer application framing for filters, couplers, power amplifiers, antennas, and satellites
- Thermal-image / high-power comparison context presented by AGC

## Why It Matters

- Adds a source-backed AGC thermally conductive low-loss RF laminate row distinct from `RF-35HTC` and generic RF-35 family references.

## Extraction Notes

- Keep the `10 GHz` Dk/Df values attached to `IPC-650 2.5.5.5.1 (Modified)`.
- Keep thermal-conductivity values tied to `125 C` and separated by unclad, `C1/C1`, and `CH/CH` constructions.
- Treat AGC comparative thermal images as manufacturer demonstration context, not as a universal finished-board power-rating claim.
- The datasheet states all test data are typical values, not specification values.
