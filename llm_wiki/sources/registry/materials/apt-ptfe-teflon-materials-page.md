## Content Summary

Ultra-low-loss PTFE (Teflon) PCB fabrication for RF and microwave to 110 GHz.

### PTFE Characteristics
- **Lowest dielectric loss**: Df as low as 0.0009 at 10 GHz
- **Dk range**: 2.1-2.6 (unfilled), up to 10.2 (ceramic-filled)
- **Commercial names**: Teflon, RT/duroid, TLY, TLX

### Processed PTFE Brands
| Brand | Key Products | Applications |
|-------|--------------|--------------|
| **Rogers** | RT/duroid 5880, 6002, RO3000 series | LEO satellite, mmWave |
| **Taconic** | TLY-5A, TLX, CER-10 | Antennas, radar |
| **Arlon** | CLTE-XT | High-performance RF |

### Special Processing Requirements
- **Plasma desmear**: CF4/O2 for PTFE hole-wall activation
- **Sodium-naphthalenide**: Wet chemistry alternative
- **Low-friction drill programs**: Prevent smearing
- **Controlled temperature**: Critical for lamination
- **Hybrid stackups**: PTFE + FR-4 for cost optimization

### Specifications
| Parameter | Capability |
|-----------|------------|
| **Frequencies** | Up to 110 GHz |
| **Df (10GHz)** | 0.0009-0.002 (pure PTFE) |
| **Trace Tolerance** | ±0.5 mil |
| **Hybrid Capable** | PTFE + FR-4 |
| **Validation** | TDR/VNA |

### Applications
- Ka-band transceivers
- 77 GHz ADAS radar
- mmWave antennas
- LEO satellite communications
- High-frequency test equipment
