## Key Specifications

### RO4000 Series (FR-4 Compatible)
- RO4350B: Dk 3.48, Df 0.0037, No plasma required
- RO4003C: Dk 3.38, Df 0.0027, No plasma required

### RO3000 Series (PTFE, Plasma Required)
- RO3003: Dk 3.00, Df 0.0010, 77 GHz automotive radar
- RO3006: Dk 6.15, Df 0.0020

### RT/duroid Series (Lowest Loss)
- RT/duroid 5880: Dk 2.20, Df 0.0009
- Requires plasma desmear
