# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- Electrical, thermal, mechanical, and physical properties for `N7000-3`
- Toughened polyimide laminate / prepreg positioning for severe-condition multilayers
- UL94 / IPC-4101 framing and UL file reference

## Why It Matters

- Adds a source-backed AGC toughened polyimide row distinct from `N7000-3F`, useful for high-temperature, avionics, backplane, burn-in board, and severe-condition material framing.

## Extraction Notes

- Keep `1 GHz`, `2.5 GHz`, and `10 GHz` electrical values attached to their listed methods.
- Keep N7000-3 distinct from N7000-3F; do not merge values across the two products.
- Treat avionics, petroleum, burn-in, and multilayer wording as material-positioning context, not finished-board qualification evidence.
- The datasheet states all test data are typical values, not specification values.
