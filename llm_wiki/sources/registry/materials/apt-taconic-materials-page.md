### Processing Requirements

- PTFE materials require plasma desmear for via reliability
- Hybrid stack-ups supported (Taconic/FR-4 via fastRise 27)
- Plasma etch workflows validated
- TDR/VNA impedance validation available

### Comparison Data

RF-35A2 vs Rogers RO4350B:
- Dk: 3.50 vs 3.48 (essentially identical)
- Df: 0.0018 vs 0.0037 (RF-35 51% lower loss)
- Thermal conductivity: Matched at 0.62 W/m·K
- CTE Z: 30 vs 32 ppm/°C (RF-35 slightly better)
- Processing: PTFE plasma required vs FR-4 chemistry
- Cost: RF-35 10-15% lower
