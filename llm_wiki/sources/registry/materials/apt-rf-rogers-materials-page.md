### Manufacturing Capabilities
- **Frequency range**: Sub-6 GHz to 86 GHz+
- **Hybrid stack-ups**: RO4000 + FR-4 (cost optimization)
- **Plasma desmear**: Dedicated PTFE processing
- **Trace tolerance**: ±0.5 mil
- **Layer count**: 4-40 layers
- **Validation**: TDR/VNA every build
- **Copper options**: Standard, VLP, HVLP

### Quality Assurance
- Authorized Rogers material sourcing
- Local stocking (RO4350B, RO4003C, RO3003)
- Rapid prototyping support
- TDR coupons on every controlled-impedance build
- IPC-6012 Class 3 acceptance
