## Content Summary

Panasonic Megtron family - dominant high-speed laminates for SerDes backplanes.

### Megtron Portfolio

| Grade | Df (1GHz) | Applications | Key Features |
|-------|-----------|--------------|--------------|
| **Megtron 4** | ~0.005 | PCIe Gen3/4, 10-25G | Low-loss FR-4 alternative |
| **Megtron 6** | ~0.002 | 56G PAM4 backplanes | Industry benchmark |
| **Megtron 7** | ~0.0015 | High-layer backplanes | Improved Z-axis CTE |
| **Megtron U** | ~0.001 | 112G/224G PAM4 | Next-generation channels |
| **Megtron 8** | Lower | 224G+ | Ultra-high-speed |

### Key Capabilities
- 56G and 112G PAM4 channel proven
- Spread-glass inventory (1035, 1067, 2116)
- VLP/HVLP copper foils stocked
- 40+ layer backplane capability
- IST-verified microvia reliability
- Sequential lamination expertise
- COM/eye-diagram analysis
- OIF-CEI-56G and IEEE 802.3ck compliant

### Design Support
- Causal RLGC modeling
- Polar Si9000e impedance simulations
- Per-layer dielectric documentation
- Copper roughness data provided
- TDR/VNA validation included

### Applications
- Data center switch fabrics
- 56G/112G SerDes backplanes
- High-performance computing
- Telecommunications infrastructure
- AI/ML accelerator boards
