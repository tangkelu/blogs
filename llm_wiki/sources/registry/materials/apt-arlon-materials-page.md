### Stocked Materials at APTPCB

- 33N: 10-60 mil, 1/2 oz and 1 oz ED copper
- 45N: 5-31 mil
- CLTE-XT: 10-60 mil

### Processing Requirements

- Polyimide (33N, 35N, 45N, Thermount): Standard FR-4 desmear, controlled press profiles, strict moisture-control baking
- PTFE (CLTE, TC, AD, CuClad): Plasma desmear required
- Thermount: Specialized routing/drilling for aramid fiber

### Operating Temperature Range

- Polyimide series: -55°C to +260°C

### Detailed Properties Available

- 33N (Polyimide), 45N (Epoxy), 55NT (Thermount), CLTE-XT (PTFE), TC350 (PTFE), AD250 (PTFE) full specifications
- Dk/Df at 1 MHz (polyimide) or 10 GHz (PTFE)
- Thermal conductivity (ASTM E1461)
- CTE X/Y/Z (IPC-TM-650 2.4.41)
- Moisture absorption (IPC-TM-650 2.6.2.1)
- Peel strength (IPC-TM-650 2.4.8)
- Tg (IPC-TM-650 2.4.25)
- Dielectric strength (IPC-TM-650 2.5.6.2)
- Flammability (UL 94)
