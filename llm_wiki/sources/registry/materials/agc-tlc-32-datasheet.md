## What It Covers

- Electrical, thermal, mechanical, arc resistance, flammability, thickness, and sheet-size context for TLC-32
- Microwave laminate positioning with Dk / Df values tied to separate test frequencies

## Why It Matters

- Keep Df tied to `10 GHz` and modified IPC-650 `2.5.5.5.1`.
