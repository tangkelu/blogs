## Content Summary

APTPCB X-Ray Inspection Services for non-destructive internal verification of PCBs and PCBAs, detecting hidden defects.

### X-Ray Inspection Types

- **2D X-ray**: Flat top-down view, quick checks, solder bridging identification
- **2.5D X-ray**: Multiple angled 2D images, enhanced depth perception, hidden joint inspection (effective for BGAs)
- **3D CT (Computed Tomography)**: Full 3D model, virtual cross-sectioning without physical damage, comprehensive analysis

### Value Areas

- HDI boards with microvias, stacked vias, ultra-fine traces
- Multilayer PCBs with complex internal routing
- Boards with BGA and hidden-joint packages
- Miniaturized and compact electronics (wearables, IoT)

### Defect Detection Capabilities

**Solder Joint Defects**:
- Voiding (reduces mechanical strength, increases thermal resistance, thermal runaway risk)

**Open Circuits**:
- Lifted pads, incomplete wetting, micro-cracks (often invisible to AOI/functional test)

**Short Circuits**:
- Internal bridging, microscopic solder whiskers under BGA/QFN, copper debris, plating defects

**Component Issues**:
- Misaligned BGAs, bottom-terminated component shifts, tombstoning, insufficient solder volume

**Internal Layer Defects**:
- Trace breaks, delamination, voids, structural imperfections, via barrel separation, incomplete plating

### Inspection Process

1. Sample preparation and precise positioning
2. Parameter setting (voltage, current, exposure)
3. Scanning with systematic X-ray beam movement
4. Image processing with advanced enhancement algorithms
5. Inspection by trained operators (AI-augmented)
6. Reporting with annotated images and defect classifications

### Quality Integration

- NPI turnaround: 24-48 hours
- Part of inspection chain: AOI + X-Ray + ICT/FCT
- Process control with SPI feedback loop
- Defect tracking with PPM monitoring
- Traceability: Barcode + lot
- IPC-A-610 Class 2/3
