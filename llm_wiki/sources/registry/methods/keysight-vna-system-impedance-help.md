## What It Covers

- VNA system-impedance setting context

## Why It Matters

- The `50-ohm` PCB drafts need a primary instrument-vendor source showing that `50 ohm` is a dominant measurement reference context, not a universal PCB design law.

## Extraction Notes

- Safe for VNA measurement-reference vocabulary.
