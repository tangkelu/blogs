# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- Bend ratio definition as bend radius divided by circuit thickness
- Static versus dynamic flexing context
- Example minimum bend ratios for single-layer, double-sided, and multilayer flex circuits
- Guidance to consult experienced flex manufacturers when designs fall below published standards

## Why It Matters

- It helps `llm_wiki` split bend-ratio design guidance from rigid-flex reliability proof.
- It provides a design-guide anchor for explaining why layer count, thickness, bend type, forming, and constraint after forming matter.
