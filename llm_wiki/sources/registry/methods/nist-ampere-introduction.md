# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- the ampere as the SI base unit of electric current
- electric current as charge in motion per unit time
- one ampere as one coulomb of charge moving past a point in one second

## Why It Matters

- Strengthens the unit-identity layer for `current` and `ampere` beyond a table-only citation.
