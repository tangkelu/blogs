## Content Summary

### BGA/QFN Fine Pitch Assembly

Specialized assembly for advanced packages requiring precision placement and inspection.

**Capabilities**:
- BGA pitch down to 0.3mm
- Micro-BGA and CSP handling
- QFN/DFN packages
- X-Ray inspection for hidden joints
- Rework and repair capabilities
- Profile development for complex thermal requirements

**Process Control**:
- Stencil design optimization for fine pitch
- Solder paste selection for small apertures
- Reflow profiling per device type
- X-Ray verification of ball alignment
- Void analysis and control

### BGA Reballing

Removal and replacement of BGA solder balls for rework or device recovery.

**Services**:
- BGA removal from PCB
- Site preparation and cleaning
- Reballing with new solder spheres
- Re-placement and reflow
- X-Ray verification after rework
- Component salvage/recovery

**Applications**:
- Defective component replacement
- Engineering changes
- Device recovery for reuse
- Upgrades and modifications
- Failed component analysis

**Standards**:
- IPC-A-610 Class 2/3
- IPC-7711/7721 rework standards
- X-Ray inspection per IPC
