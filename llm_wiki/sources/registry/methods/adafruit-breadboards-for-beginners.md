# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- Breadboard basics for beginners
- Adafruit's explanation of `Perma-Proto` boards as a way to transfer a temporary breadboarded design into a soldered layout with similar hole pattern
- Educational framing around moving from temporary experimentation toward a more stable prototype

## Why It Matters

- It adds a concrete public education anchor for the temporary-versus-more-permanent prototype distinction.
- It is useful for explaining why breadboard-like soldered transfer boards exist without forcing unsupported universal naming claims.

## Extraction Notes

- Safe for temporary solderless prototyping and more permanent soldered-transfer-board contrast.
- Treat `Perma-Proto` as a brand-specific example, not as proof that all `protoboard`, `perfboard`, or `stripboard` terms are equivalent.
- Do not infer durability, signal-integrity, current, or frequency rankings from this guide alone.
