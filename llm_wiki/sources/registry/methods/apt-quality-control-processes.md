## Content Summary

### First Article Inspection (FAI)

Comprehensive verification of initial production units.

**Scope**:
- BOM verification
- Component placement accuracy
- Solder joint quality
- Dimensional checks
- Electrical verification
- Documentation review
- Process validation

### Incoming Quality Control (IQC)

Material and component verification before production.

**Checks**:
- Component authenticity
- Packaging integrity
- Label verification
- Quantity confirmation
- Visual inspection
- Sample testing
- Certificate of conformance review

### Final Quality Inspection (FQI)

Pre-shipment verification of finished PCBAs.

**Inspections**:
- Visual inspection per IPC-A-610
- Dimensional verification
- Functional sampling
- Packaging verification
- Label and documentation check
- Cosmetic inspection

### Quality System

Overall quality management framework.

**Systems**:
- ISO 9001:2015 certified
- IPC-A-610 Class 2/3 compliance
- Traceability systems
- Document control
- Corrective action processes
- Continuous improvement
- Supplier quality management
- Customer complaint handling

**Metrics**:
- Defect tracking with PPM monitoring
- First-pass yield tracking
- On-time delivery
- Customer satisfaction
- Internal audit results
