## What It Covers

- Flex-circuit design recommendations
- Bend-radius guidance for double-layer and multilayer circuits
- Material and construction considerations for reliability, including thickness, adhesiveless materials, copper plating in bend areas, and unbonding
- General DFM considerations for flex-circuit design

- Gives rigid-flex and flex articles a public design-guidance source for why bend ratio depends on thickness, layer count, construction, and whether the bend is static or dynamic.
