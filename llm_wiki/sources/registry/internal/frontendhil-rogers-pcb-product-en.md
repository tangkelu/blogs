### Manufacturing Process

- Plasma activation for PTFE adhesion (>40 dynes/cm)
- Low-profile/VLP copper for reduced conductor loss (10-25%)
- Staged pressure/temperature lamination
- Controlled-depth drilling for launch transitions
- Backdrill optimization for low reflection
- TDR-verified coupons
- VNA S-parameters (S11/S21) to 40 GHz
- Documentation aligned with IPC-6018

### Hybrid Stackup Strategy

- RF-critical layers on Rogers
- Internal planes on multilayer FR-4
- Cost reduction: 30-50% vs full Rogers
