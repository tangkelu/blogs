### Manufacturing Processes

**DBC (Direct Bond Copper)**:
- High-temperature bonding of copper foils to ceramic
- Bond strength: peel tests >1.4 N/mm
- Low thermal resistance for power modules

**DPC (Direct Plate Copper)**:
- Thin-film deposition for precision
- Fine-line control: 50/50 μm
- RF microstrip and coplanar lines

**LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic)**:
- Multilayer ceramics with embedded conductors/vias
- Compact RF modules
- Co-fired integration

### Quality Control

- SPC monitoring: copper thickness, adhesion, via quality
- TDR/VNA for RF impedance (±5%)
- Thermal shock validation (−55↔+250°C)
- FEA stress modeling for CTE mismatch
- Bond pull and shear tests
