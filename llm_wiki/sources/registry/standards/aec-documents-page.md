# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- Official AEC document hub
- AEC-Q document-family discovery for automotive electronic component qualification

## Why It Matters

- ADAS / ECU drafts often misuse AEC-Q as if it directly qualifies a PCB or PCBA. This source keeps AEC language at document-family / component-qualification scope.

## Extraction Notes

- Safe for AEC-Q document-family identity.
- Do not infer PCB, PCBA, supplier, lot, or system qualification from the document hub alone.
