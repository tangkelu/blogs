# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- official IEC metadata for `IEC 62052-11:2020`
- exact title: `Electricity metering equipment - General requirements, tests and test conditions - Part 11: Metering equipment`
- public scope stating that the document specifies requirements, tests, and test conditions for type testing of AC and DC electricity meters
- public note that tampering is not covered and that meter safety requirements were removed to `IEC 62052-31:2015`

## Why It Matters

- It anchors the exact `IEC 62052-11` noun used in the `smart-meter` draft and clarifies that this is a type-testing and metering-equipment document family, not a generic safety or anti-tamper proof page.
