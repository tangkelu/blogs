# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- USB-IF Qualification by Similarity program context
- Product categories that may be handled through similarity review in the USB-IF ecosystem
- Explicit program context that QbS is not intended to remove the need for compliance or EU conformity testing

## Why It Matters

- Helps prevent public copy from implying that a similar Type-C charger, connector, cable, or board design is automatically certified.

## Extraction Notes

- Safe for QbS program vocabulary and the non-claim boundary that similarity is not a blanket test waiver.
- Do not infer customer-specific eligibility, certification status, EU conformity status, or lab outcome.

- Refresh before publication when using QbS or EU conformity wording.
