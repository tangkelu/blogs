# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- Official IPC product metadata for `IPC-6012FS`
- Public statement that the addendum provides exceptions to `IPC Performance Class 3` requirements of `IPC-6012F`
- Public framing that it addresses space and military avionics product, including production-lot acceptance-testing changes and survival under vibration, thermal cycling, and ground testing

## Why It Matters

- It is the strongest current official product-page anchor in this corpus for showing that space/military rigid-board work is handled through a program-specific addendum layer rather than through generic `Class 3` wording alone.
