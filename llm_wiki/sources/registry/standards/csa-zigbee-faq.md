- CSA FAQ-level Zigbee technology and certification context

## Extraction Notes

- Safe to cite Zigbee as a CSA technology built on IEEE 802.15.4 and to cite CSA's bridge boundary between Zigbee and Matter.
- Exact raw throughput, frequency, range, node-count, encryption, Zigbee PRO release, certification, and market-size claims require careful citation to the exact FAQ wording and refresh before publication.
- Do not use this source alone to claim any specific product, board, hub, USB coordinator, or supplier is Zigbee-certified, interoperable, secure, reliable, or suitable for a customer application.
