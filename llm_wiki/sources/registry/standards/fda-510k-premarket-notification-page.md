# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- FDA 510(k) premarket notification program for medical devices
- Substantial equivalence determination process
- Device clearance (not approval) pathway

## Why It Matters

- Medical device PCB content sometimes references FDA 510(k) for regulatory context
- Important to separate device clearance vocabulary from PCB/supplier approval claims

## Extraction Notes

- Safe for 510(k) program identity and medical device clearance vocabulary
- Do not claim "510(k) approved PCB", "FDA cleared board", or supplier/device approval
- 510(k) is device-level clearance, not PCB-level certification
