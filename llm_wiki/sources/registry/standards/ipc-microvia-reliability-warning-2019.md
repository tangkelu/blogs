# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- IPC public warning that post-fabrication microvia failures had been observed in high-performance products
- Public statement that traditional lot-acceptance and microsection-only inspection can miss latent weak-interface microvia failures
- Public context for IPC's shift toward stronger performance-based acceptance thinking

## Why It Matters

- It is one of the strongest public IPC anchors for discussing why stacked-microvia and high-density interconnect reliability claims should be handled carefully in advanced multilayer content.

## Extraction Notes

- Safe for high-level reliability-risk framing and for caution against overclaiming microvia robustness
- Do not infer universal failure rates, qualification thresholds, or mandatory test procedures from this notice alone
