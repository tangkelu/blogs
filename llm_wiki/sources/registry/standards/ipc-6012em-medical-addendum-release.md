# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- Official IPC announcement for `IPC-6012EM`
- Public statement that the addendum was created for medical-device printed boards needing more stringent fabrication requirements than current `IPC Class 3`
- Public framing that `IPC-6012EM` introduces a medical-device-oriented design level `D`

## Why It Matters

- It is the safest public IPC anchor in this corpus for saying that medical-device board programs may use a medical addendum layer beyond generic `Class 3` vocabulary without reproducing controlled standard text.
