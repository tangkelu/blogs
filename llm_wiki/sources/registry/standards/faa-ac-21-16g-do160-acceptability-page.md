# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- official FAA identity for `AC 21-16G`
- FAA statement that RTCA `DO-160` versions `D`, `E`, `F`, and `G` contain acceptable environmental qualifications for showing compliance with certain airworthiness requirements
- FAA statement strongly encouraging use of `RTCA/DO-160G` for new articles

## Why It Matters

- It gives an official regulator anchor for treating `DO-160G` as an aviation environmental-qualification document family rather than as casual marketing shorthand.
