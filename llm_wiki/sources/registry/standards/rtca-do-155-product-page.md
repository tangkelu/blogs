# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- official RTCA metadata for `DO-155`
- document title `Minimum Performance Standards-Airborne Low Range Radar Altimeters`
- high-level description that the document recommends standards and test procedures for characteristics essential to low-range radar-altimeter operation for measured height above terrain
- issue date and committee metadata

## Why It Matters

- It gives a standards-owner anchor that `DO-155` is specific to airborne low-range radar altimeters, not to barometric or laser altimeters in general.
