# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- Official identity and revision metadata for `AS6171A`
- High-level scope for inspection and test procedures used when parts have unknown chain of custody or elevated counterfeit risk
- Public statement that no amount of testing can confirm authenticity without a known chain of custody to the original manufacturer or authorized source

## Why It Matters

- It is the strongest current public anchor in this corpus for resisting overclaims that inspection or screening alone proves authenticity in high-reliability supply chains.
