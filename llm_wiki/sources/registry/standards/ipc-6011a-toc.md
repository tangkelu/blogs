# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- Official IPC `IPC-6011A` table of contents PDF
- Public identity for `IPC-6011A` as `Generic Performance Specification for Printed Boards`
- Public scope statement that it establishes generic requirements for printed boards associated with the `IPC-601X` series, plus public TOC coverage for qualification assessment, quality assurance, traceability, document control, acceptance/rejected lots, and periodic quality conformance testing

## Why It Matters

- It gives `llm_wiki` a stronger public hierarchy above sectional `IPC-601X` documents, so `22-layer` writing can separate generic printed-board framework language from sectional/addendum claims and from supplier-status claims.
