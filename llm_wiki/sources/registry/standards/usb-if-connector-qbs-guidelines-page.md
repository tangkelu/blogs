# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- USB-IF Qualification by Similarity guidance for USB connectors and cable assemblies
- General indication that changes to connectors or cable assemblies can require test-group review
- USB Type-C connector and cable assembly categories within the QbS guidance

## Why It Matters

- Supports conservative wording that Type-C connector substitutions or mechanical changes should be treated as controlled review items, not casual BOM alternates.

## Extraction Notes

- Safe for QbS context, connector-change sensitivity, and the idea that test requirements depend on the nature and magnitude of changes.
- Do not claim a product qualifies by similarity or needs limited/no testing without program-specific evidence.
