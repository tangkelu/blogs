# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- FDA MRI risk context
- MR labeling vocabulary including `MR Safe`, `MR Conditional`, and `MR Unsafe`
- Device-level hazard framing around magnetic fields, RF energy, implants, and conditions of safe use

## Why It Matters

- MRI and pacemaker PCB drafts must not claim `MRI-compatible PCB` or implant suitability from board materials or workmanship alone.
