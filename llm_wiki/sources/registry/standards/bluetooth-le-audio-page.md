# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- Official Bluetooth SIG overview page for `LE Audio`
- High-level SIG framing that LE Audio enhances Bluetooth audio, adds support for hearing aids, and introduces `Auracast` broadcast audio
- Product-category vocabulary for Bluetooth audio built on Bluetooth Low Energy

## Why It Matters

- Keeps hearing-aid connectivity drafts anchored to Bluetooth SIG naming and product-level scope instead of implying that a component, PCB, or device is certified or interoperable by default.

## Extraction Notes

- Safe for `LE Audio` identity, Bluetooth SIG naming, and broad feature-family framing.
