# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- Official IPC Validation Services page for the `Qualified Products List (QPL) IPC-4103`
- Public framing that `IPC-4103` covers base materials and bonding-layer materials for high-speed / high-frequency printed-board applications
- Public metadata that qualification/listing can attach to listed `IPC-4103` products and the manufacturing sites where those products are produced

## Why It Matters

- It adds a more specific official boundary than `IPC-4101 QPL` alone by showing that IPC qualification-listing language can also sit at the high-speed/high-frequency base-material and bonding-layer layer, not at the finished-board layer.

## Extraction Notes

- Safe for `IPC-4103` high-speed/high-frequency base-material, bonding-layer, product-listing, and site-scope vocabulary
- Do not infer finished-board qualification, interconnect qualification, channel-performance qualification, supplier approval, or customer acceptance from this page alone
