# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- FDA Premarket Approval (PMA) program for Class III medical devices
- Most stringent device marketing application type
- Scientific and regulatory review for safety and effectiveness

## Why It Matters

- High-risk medical device PCB content may reference PMA
- Important to separate device approval vocabulary from PCB-level claims

## Extraction Notes

- Safe for PMA program identity and Class III device approval vocabulary
- Do not claim "PMA-approved PCB", "FDA approved board", or supplier/device approval at PCB level
- PMA is device-level approval, not PCB or supplier certification

- Refresh before publication if citing specific PMA submission or approval status
