# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- official IEC metadata for `IEC 62053-23:2020`
- exact title: `Electricity metering equipment - Particular requirements - Part 23: Static meters for reactive energy (classes 2 and 3)`
- public scope stating that the document applies to static var-hour meters and to their type tests only
- public notes that tampering is not covered, safety requirements are covered in `IEC 62052-31:2015`, and `IEC 62053-23` should not be used for new meter designs because the reactive-energy classes were added to `IEC 62053-24`

## Why It Matters

- It supports guarded use of `IEC 62053-23` as a reactive-energy document-family noun while keeping it distinct from active-energy meter claims.
