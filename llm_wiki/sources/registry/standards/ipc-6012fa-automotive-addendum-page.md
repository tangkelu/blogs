# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- Official IPC product metadata for `IPC-6012FA`
- Public statement that the addendum, when required by procurement documentation/drawings, supplements or replaces specifically identified requirements of `IPC-6012F`
- Public framing that the addendum applies to rigid printed boards that must survive vibration and thermal-cycling environments in automotive interconnect use

## Why It Matters

- It is the strongest current official product-page anchor in this corpus for treating automotive hi-rel rigid-board requirements as a program-specific addendum layer rather than as generic `IPC-6012F` or generic `Class 3` wording.
