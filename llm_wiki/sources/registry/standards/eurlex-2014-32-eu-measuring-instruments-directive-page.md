# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- official EUR-Lex page for `Directive 2014/32/EU`
- public directive scope covering measuring instruments in instrument-specific annexes, including `active electrical energy meters (MI-003)`
- public MI-003 framing for active electrical energy meters intended for residential, commercial, and light industrial use
- public conformity-assessment routes listed for MI-003
