# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- Official IEC metadata for `IEC 61300-3-35:2022`
- Visual inspection scope for fibre optic connectors and fibre-stub transceivers
- Public description that the document addresses observation and classification of debris, scratches, and defects
- Public statement that inspection includes contamination review across the whole contact area

## Why It Matters

- This is the current standards-owner anchor for safe wording around visual inspection of optical connector end faces and fibre-stub transceiver interfaces.
- It gives a reusable boundary that inspection and contamination review exist, while also limiting overreach into optical performance claims.
