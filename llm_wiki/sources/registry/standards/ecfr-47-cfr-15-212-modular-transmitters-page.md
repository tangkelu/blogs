# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- FCC modular-transmitter requirements under 47 CFR 15.212
- Boundary between approved transmitter module and host device obligations
- Labeling, antenna, shielding, and instruction obligations attached to modular approval

## Why It Matters

- Supports cautious wording that a pre-approved radio module changes the certification route, but does not erase host-device review duties.

## Extraction Notes

- Safe for guarded statements that modular approval has conditions around antenna use, labeling, instructions, and final configuration responsibility.
- Safe for wording that the host product still needs its integration reviewed rather than assuming the PCB is automatically cleared.
