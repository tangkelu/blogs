# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- Official IEC metadata for `IEC TR 62627-01:2023`
- Fibre optic connector cleaning methods, including tools, machines, and cleaning procedures
- Public description that the document covers connector plugs, adaptors, receptacles excluding optical transceivers, and dust caps
- Public statement that visual inspection procedures are covered separately in `IEC 61300-3-35`

## Why It Matters

- This is a current official authority for narrow contamination-control wording centered on connector cleaning workflows rather than broad optical-module claims.
- It supports conservative guidance language for instruction-manual or maintenance-style handling context.

## Extraction Notes

- Safe for `cleaning methods`, `cleaning tools`, `cleaning procedures`, `reasons for inspection and cleaning`, and covered hardware-family vocabulary.
- Safe for saying receptacle-style optical transceiver cleaning is handled separately in `IEC TR 62572-4`.
- Do not use this page alone for visual inspection criteria, acceptance thresholds, contamination limits, or optical performance outcomes.
- Do not generalize this connector-focused report into universal optical-module manufacturing cleanliness, non-outgassing, or release claims.
