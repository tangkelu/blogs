## Content Summary

### Selective Soldering

Precision soldering for through-hole components on mixed-technology boards.

**Key Capabilities**:
- Selective solder fountain for THT components
- Nitrogen inerting for improved joint quality
- Mini-wave and dedicated nozzle options
- For mixed SMT/THT assemblies
- Temperature profiling
- Flux application control

**Applications**:
- Connectors requiring through-hole strength
- High-power components
- Transformers and inductors
- Mixed-technology assemblies

### Conformal Coating

Protective coating services for environmental protection.

**Key Capabilities**:
- Acrylic, urethane, silicone, epoxy coatings
- Spray, brush, dip application methods
- Selective coating (masking capabilities)
- UV inspection for coverage verification
- Humidity and contamination protection

**Coating Types**:
| Type | Properties | Applications |
|------|------------|--------------|
| Acrylic | Easy repair, good moisture resistance | General purpose |
| Urethane | Chemical resistant, abrasion resistant | Harsh environments |
| Silicone | High temp, flexible | Thermal cycling |
| Epoxy | Hard, chemical resistant | Maximum protection |

**Standards**:
- IPC-A-610 Class 2/3
- IPC-CC-830 coating qualification
