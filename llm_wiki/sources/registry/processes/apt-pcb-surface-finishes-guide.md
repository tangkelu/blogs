## Content Summary

Complete PCB surface finish portfolio with XRF-verified thickness control.

### Available Finishes

| Finish | Composition | Applications | Shelf Life |
|--------|-------------|--------------|------------|
| **LF-HASL** | Lead-free solder | Cost-sensitive, large components | 12 months |
| **ENIG** | Electroless Ni / Immersion Au | Fine pitch, wire bonding | 12 months |
| **ENEPIG** | Electroless Ni / Electroless Pd / Immersion Au | Universal, aluminum wire bond | 12 months |
| **Immersion Silver** | Ag plating | RF/High-frequency | 6 months |
| **Immersion Tin** | Sn plating | Press-fit connectors | 6 months |
| **OSP** | Organic solderability preservative | Low-cost, single-side SMT | 6 months |
| **Hard Gold** | Electrolytic Au (Ni underplate) | Edge connectors, wear surfaces | 24+ months |

### Quality Control
- XRF (X-Ray Fluorescence) thickness verification
- IPC J-STD-003 solderability qualification
- Dedicated chemistry lines per finish type
- Every production lot tested

### Selection Guide

| Application | Recommended Finish |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Fine pitch (<0.5mm) | ENIG, ENEPIG |
| BGA/CSP | ENIG, ENEPIG |
| Wire bonding | ENIG, ENEPIG |
| RF/Microwave | Immersion Silver, ENIG |
| Edge connectors | Hard Gold |
| Press-fit | Immersion Tin, ENEPIG |
| Cost sensitive | OSP, LF-HASL |

### Conformal Coating Options
- **Acrylic**: Easy repair, general moisture protection
- **Urethane**: Chemical/abrasion resistant
- **Silicone**: High temperature, flexible
- **Epoxy**: Maximum protection, hard coating
