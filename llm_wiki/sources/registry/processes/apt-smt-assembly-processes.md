## Content Summary

### SMT (Surface Mount Technology) Assembly

High-volume SMT assembly with full process control.

**Capabilities**:
- 7 SMT production lines
- Component sizes: 01005 to large QFPs/BGAs
- BGA pitch down to 0.3mm
- 01005 micro-component placement
- X-Ray inspection for BGA/QFN
- Reflow profiling per board

### THT (Through-Hole Technology) Assembly

Traditional through-hole assembly for power and high-reliability components.

**Capabilities**:
- Wave soldering
- Selective soldering
- Hand soldering for complex/rare components
- Axial and radial component handling
- High-power component expertise

### Turnkey Assembly

Complete PCBA solution from components to finished assembly.

**Services**:
- Component sourcing and procurement
- PCB fabrication coordination
- Full assembly (SMT + THT)
- Testing (ICT, FCT, flying probe)
- Box build and system integration
- Packaging and logistics

### Mass Production

High-volume production capabilities with quality systems.

**Capabilities**:
- Scalable production lines
- Process control and SPC
- Quality systems and traceability
- Supplier management
- ESD protection
- Continuous improvement

**Quality Integration** (all processes):
- SPI → AOI → X-Ray → ICT/FCT inspection chain
- Process control with feedback loops
- Defect tracking with PPM monitoring
- Traceability: Barcode + lot
- IPC-A-610 Class 2/3
