## Content Summary

### PCB Stencil (SMT Stencil)

Stainless steel laser-cut stencils for solder paste printing.

**Capabilities**:
- Laser-cut stainless steel stencils
- Step stencils for mixed-component boards
- Nano-coating for improved paste release
- Frameless and framed options
- Electropolished finish
- Step-down areas for connector keepouts

**Design Features**:
- Aperture design per IPC-7525
- Aspect ratio optimization
- Area ratio calculations
- Fiducial integration
- Multi-level thickness (step stencils)
- Component-specific aperture shapes

### Support Services

Engineering and technical support for PCBA projects.

**Services**:
- DFM (Design for Manufacturing) review
- DFT (Design for Test) consultation
- Stack-up engineering
- Impedance calculation and modeling
- Test strategy development
- Process optimization
- Failure analysis support
- Technical documentation

### Testing & Quality Services

Comprehensive testing and quality assurance.

**Testing Options**:
- AOI (Automated Optical Inspection)
- SPI (Solder Paste Inspection)
- X-Ray inspection
- ICT (In-Circuit Test)
- FCT (Functional Circuit Test)
- Flying probe test
- Environmental testing (on request)

**Quality Documentation**:
- First Article Inspection (FAI)
- Test reports
- Process validation reports
- Traceability records
- Certificate of conformance
