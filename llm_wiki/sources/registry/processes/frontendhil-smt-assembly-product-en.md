# Source Summary

HIL SMT assembly service capabilities for prototype to production.

## Key Capabilities

### Placement Accuracy
- **Standard**: ±25 μm @ 3σ
- **Advanced**: ±8 μm @ 3σ

### Component Sizes
- **Standard**: 0201 (0.6 × 0.3 mm)
- **Advanced**: 01005 / 008004 (0.4 × 0.2 mm / 0.25 × 0.125 mm)

### Fine-Pitch
- Down to 0.2 mm BGA/CSP

### Solder Paste Control
- Stencil: 100–150 μm thick
- Volume control: ±10%
- Transfer efficiency: ~95–100%

### Inspection
- 3D SPI (Solder Paste Inspection)
- 3D AOI (Automated Optical Inspection)
- Sample X-Ray for hidden joints (BGA/QFN)
- AOI true-defect detection: >95%

### Reflow
- Nitrogen reflow option
- Profile optimization with thermocouple mapping
- Reflow zones: Ramp, soak, TAL, peak

### Quality Metrics
- **FPY**: ≥98% (First Pass Yield)
- **DPPM**: <500 (Defects Per Million)

### Traceability
- MES (Manufacturing Execution System) traceability
- Paste lot traceability
- SPI-to-AOI correlation
- Digital profile logging per lot

### Assembly Types
- SMT, Through-Hole, Mixed Technology
- Double-sided SMT, PoP, SiP

### Additional Services
- Selective solder and wave solder
- Underfill/conformal coating
- Ionic contamination control: ≤1.56 μg/cm²

### Standards
- IPC-A-610
- J-STD-001
