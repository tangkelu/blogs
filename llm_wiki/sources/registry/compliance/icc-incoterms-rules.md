# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- Incoterms rules as eleven three-letter trade terms for business-to-business contracts for sale and purchase of goods.
- ICC framing that the rules help clarify delivery-related tasks, costs, and risks between seller and buyer.
- Current ICC page statement that `Incoterms 2020` entered into force on `1 January 2020`.

## Why It Matters

- PCB procurement and shipping articles often confuse trade-term allocation with manufacturing lead time or customs-clearance guarantees.
- This source supports only the trade-term allocation boundary.

## Extraction Notes

- Safe for contract and logistics governance vocabulary.
- Do not infer duties, taxes, HS codes, customs clearance speed, delivery dates, or board-factory performance from Incoterms.
