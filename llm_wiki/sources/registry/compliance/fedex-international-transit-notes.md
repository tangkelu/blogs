# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- Certain commodities and high-value shipments may require additional clearance time.
- Transit-time and service displays assume no clearance delays.
- Estimated delivery depends on ship date, weight, dimensions, declared value, goods description, packaging, and address data matching the tendered shipment.

## Why It Matters

- PCB shipping and quick-turn articles should keep carrier transit estimates separate from manufacturing lead time and customs risk.

## Extraction Notes

- Safe for caveating transit-time estimates.
- Do not turn rate/transit-time application output into guaranteed delivery promises.

## Refresh Notes

- Refresh before publication because carrier terms, service names, and clearance notes change.
