# Source Summary

## What It Covers

- Most international shipments are subject to customs duties and taxes.
- Duties are commonly tied to customs value and Harmonized System classification, with country-specific variations.
- Import taxes such as VAT/GST can be calculated on goods value, freight, insurance, and duties.
- Accurate waybill and customs invoice details are essential for clearance.

## Why It Matters

- PCB procurement content needs landed-cost and customs-risk language without inventing duty rates or clearance promises.
