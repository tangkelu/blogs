# Source Summary

HIL Heavy Copper PCB product specifications for high-current power electronics.

## Key Capabilities

### Copper Thickness
- **Range**: 3–20 oz (105–700 μm)
- **Standard**: 3–6 oz
- **High-power**: 10 oz or more

### Layer Count
- **Standard**: 2–8 layers
- **Advanced**: Up to 32 layers

### Current Capacity
- **Typical**: 30–50 A (traces exceed this threshold)
- **Example**: 4 oz trace at 10 mm width: 50–80 A

### Thermal Management
- **Temperature rise reduction**: 10–30 °C
- **Thermal via arrays**: Ø0.30–0.50 mm

### Plating Control
- **Uniformity**: ±10% via controlled current density
- **Pulse-reverse profiles**
- **Thickness mapping**: 25 points

### Etching
- **Differential etching**: For mixed copper weights
- **Step-down recipes**: Address undercut
- **Lateral etch ratio**: ~1:1 at extreme weights

### Materials
- **High-Tg**: 170–180 °C
- **Advanced**: High thermal conductivity FR-4, Rogers, Metal Core (IMS)

### Lamination
- **Pressure**: Up to ~500 psi
- **Warpage control**: ≤0.75%

### Validation
- IPC-2152 current analysis
- Thermal cycling validation
- High-current load testing with IR thermography
- IPC-6012 Class 2/3
- IATF 16949 / ISO 13485 capable

### Lead Time
- **Quick-turn**: 5–10 days
