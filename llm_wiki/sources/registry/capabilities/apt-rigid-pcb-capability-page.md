## Content Summary

APTPCB rigid PCB manufacturing capabilities including:
- Up to 64 layer count capability
- 2/2 mil min trace/space
- 0.15 mm mechanical drill / 0.075 mm laser drill
- 20:1 mechanical drill aspect ratio
- ±5 Ω / ±7% impedance control tolerance
- 1-3 SBU HDI capability
- 0.3 mm pitch BGA support
