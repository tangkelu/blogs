# Source Summary

HIL Metal Core PCB (MCPCB) product specifications for LEDs and power electronics.

## Key Capabilities

### Base Materials
- **Aluminum**: 5052/6061, ~140–160 W/m·K
- **Copper**: C110, ~380–400 W/m·K
- **Stainless**: Optional

### Layer Count
- **Standard**: 1–2 layers
- **Advanced**: Up to 4 layers

### System Thermal Conductivity
- 1–8 W/m·K (dielectric)

### Dielectric Thickness
- 75–150 μm (dominates thermal impedance)
- Uniformity: ±10%

### Thermal Vias
- **Diameter**: Ø0.30–0.50 mm
- **Pitch**: 1.0–1.5 mm
- Copper-filled

### Validation
- **ASTM D5470**: Thermal resistance
- **Hi-Pot**: 100% up to 4,000 V AC
- **Void area**: <2%
- **Thermal cycling**: −40°C ↔ +125°C, 500–1,000 cycles
- **Resistance change**: <10%

### Process Control
- Vacuum lamination: 20–30 kg/cm²
- Peak temperature: 175–185°C
- Peel strength: ≥1.5 N/mm
- MES batch & unit-level traceability

### Applications
- LED lighting (white solder mask >85% reflectance)
- Power electronics
- High-power MOSFETs
- Thermal management
