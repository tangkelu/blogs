# Source Summary

HIL IC Substrate PCB product specifications for advanced semiconductor packaging.

## Key Capabilities

### Technology
- **Semi-Additive Process (SAP)**: 15–20 μm line/space
- **Density gain**: 2–4× vs subtractive HDI
- **Cleanroom**: Class 1000 for SAP/mSAP lines

### Materials
- **ABF (Ajinomoto Build-up Film)**: 30–60 μm dielectric layers
- **BT Resin**: X-Y CTE 12–14 ppm/°C
  - Bridges silicon (~2.6 ppm/°C) and FR-4 (~16–18 ppm/°C)
- **CTE target**: 9–14 ppm/°C composite

### Microvias
- **Diameter**: 25–50 μm
- **Depth control**: ±5 μm
- **Stacked microvias**: Supported

### Layer Count
- **Standard**: 4–16 layers
- **Advanced**: Up to 50 layers

### Board Thickness
- **Standard**: 0.20–0.80 mm
- **Advanced**: Down to 0.10 mm

### Fine-Line Capability
- **SAP**: 15–20 μm line/space
- **Undercut**: Minimal vs subtractive
- **mSAP**: Alternative for specific applications

### Plating
- **Seed-layer uniformity**: ±10%
- **Pulse-reverse plating**: Edge growth management
- **Differential etch**: Width control ±10%

### Warpage Control
- **Target**: ≤0.5% of diagonal
- **Critical for**: Flip-chip and wire-bond

### Impedance
- **Tolerance**: ±5%

### Inspection
- **AOI resolution**: ≤5 μm
- **X-ray**: Registration targets
- **IST**: 200–500 cycles (Interconnect Stress Test)

### Applications
- Advanced packaging
- Flip-chip assemblies
- SiP (System in Package)
- Wire-bond ready
- High-density interconnects

### Standards
- JEDEC-aligned reliability
- IPC-4101/4103

### Packaging Support
- Signal integrity simulation
- Backplane correlation methods
- PDN loop inductance control
