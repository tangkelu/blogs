## Content Summary

APTPCB ceramic PCB manufacturing capabilities using DPC (Direct Plated Copper), LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic), and DBC (Direct Bonded Copper) processes. Supports Alumina (Al₂O₃) and Aluminum Nitride (AlN) substrates for high thermal conductivity applications.

## Production Technologies

- **DPC (Direct Plated Copper)**: 0.5-10 oz copper
- **LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic)**: Multi-layer ceramic
- **DBC (Direct Bonded Copper)**: High power applications
