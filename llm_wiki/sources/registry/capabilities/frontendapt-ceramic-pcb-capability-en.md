# Source Summary

APT Ceramic PCB manufacturing capabilities.

## Key Capabilities

### Materials
- **96% Alumina (Al2O3)**: 20–27 W/m·K
- **Aluminum Nitride (AlN)**: 180–220 W/m·K

### Technologies
- DPC (Direct Plated Copper)
- LTCC (Low-Temperature Co-fired Ceramic)
- DBC (Direct Bonded Copper)

### Substrate Size
- Standard: 114×114mm, 120×120mm, 140×130mm, 190×140mm
- Custom available

### Thickness
- 0.2mm, 0.25mm, 0.3mm, 0.38mm, 0.5mm, 0.635mm, 0.8mm, 1.0mm, 1.2mm, 1.5mm

### Layers
- 1 Layer, 2 Layers

### Copper Weight
- H/H through 10/10 oz (with DPC/DBC)

### Trace/Space (DPC)
- 5-10 µm Cu: 0.05/0.05 mm (2/2 mil)
- 18 µm (0.5 oz): 0.075/0.075 mm (3/3 mil)
- 35 µm (1 oz): 0.1/0.1 mm (4/4 mil)

### Drilling
- Min hole: 0.06 mm (2.4 mil)
- Tolerance: ±20%
- Shift angle: ±0.025 mm

### Laser Capabilities
- Laser drilling based on thickness
- Laser outline/cutting: Max 3.0mm thickness
- Laser scribing: ≤0.7mm depth

### Tolerances
- Outer dimensions: ≤ ±0.05 mm
- Hole-to-hole spacing: 0.15 mm (center to center)

### Applications
- Power electronics (LED, IGBT)
- Automotive (ECU, power modules)
- Medical (sensors, implantable)
- Aerospace & defense (high-temp, RF)
- High-frequency RF (antennas, filters)
