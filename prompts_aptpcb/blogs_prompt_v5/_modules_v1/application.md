## Module: Applications / Industry use cases (enable when keyword implies “applications/used in/industry”)

When this module is enabled, the article must include:

### Required industry table
Add a table:
`Industry | Typical product | Key constraint | Why {{keyword}} fits | What to verify`

Requirements:
- At least 6 rows.
- Include these industries when relevant: Automotive, Medical, Telecommunications, Industrial, Aerospace/Defense, Consumer electronics.
- “Key constraint” must be specific (e.g., EMI margin, thermal path, vibration, creepage/clearance, yield, traceability).
- “What to verify” must be an acceptance/verification action (test/inspection/criteria), not marketing.

### Required content
- “Selection criteria by use case” section: 6–10 decision rules.
- Add at least 2 real-world failure modes that appear in those industries (symptom → cause → prevention).

### Tone guardrails
- Avoid generic “benefits” lists; connect every benefit to a measurable constraint or test.
